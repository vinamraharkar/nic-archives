## Computerized Electricity Billing at Jammu

Minister for Power, J&K Government, Sh. Nawang Rigzin Jora inaugurated a Computerized Electricity Billing Centre at Jammu on 15th August 2007. This is the first Computerized Electricity Billing Centre commissioned in Jammu and Kashmir. Also present on the occasion were MLA East, Sh. Yogesh Sawhney, Chief Engineer PDD, Sh. D.S Pawar, SIO, Sh. Abhay Kumar, Project Coordinator of NIC J&K Sh. J.S. Modi and other senior officers of the department. With this Centre, the Power Development Department, J&K will have a fully Computerized in-house Electricity Bill Generation facility available with the help of JKRMCE (J&K Revenue Management through Computerized Electricity Billing), a software developed by NIC, J&K.
