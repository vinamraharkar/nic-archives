## New Website Launch: NIC Maharashtra

Hon'ble Chief Minister of Maharashtra, Shri Vilas Rao Deshmukh inaugurated the website containing the details of the activities of NIC, Maharashtra State Centre. The site can be accessed on http://maharashtra.nic.in
