## Bilaspur District, HP

The Bilaspur distt of Himachal Pradesh recently went online with the launch of its website by the Hon’ble Chief Minister of HP. The website, developed by NIC Bilaspur District Centre provides important information relating to historical and religious places, geographical conditions, fairs, festivals, administration, bus routes, time tables, development and welfare schemes etc. A section of the site has been exclusively dedicated to the freedom fighters and martyrs belonging to the district.
