## TRAININGS ORGANIZED

New Delhi: As many as forty training programmes in computers and network utilization were organized by NIC in the course of one year, starting from October 1996, for personnel of the Ministry of Labour. About 300 Ministry personnel have so far been benefitted by these programmes. Conducted with active co-operation from the Career and Management Training (CMT) of the Labour Ministry, this array of training programmes was a practical sequel to the implementation of a Local Area Network (LAN) in the Ministry.
