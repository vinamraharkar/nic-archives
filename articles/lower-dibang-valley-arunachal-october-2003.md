## Lower Dibang Valley, Arunachal

The Official Website of Lower Dibang Valley District was recently inaugurated by Sh.Roding Pertin, Hon’ble Minister for Environment & Forest, Government of Arunachal Pradesh at a special function in the presence of Deputy Commissioner, all Heads of Departments, Zilla Parishad Chairman, Anchal Samity Chairman, all public leaders and other officials. All visitors showed great enthusiasm to see the website which has plenty of information about the District, its history, archaeology, flora and fauna as well as citizen services being provided by the Administration. http://roing.nic.in
