## IT Training Drive for Rural Youth in Bhiwani

District Administration Bhiwani, under the aegis of District IT Society and in collaboration with NIC Bhiwani, has launched a special drive to impart Basic Computer Training to Rural Youth. Each Tehsil and Sub Tehsil Center in the district will enroll 20 students for a period of 2 months to impart Basic IT Training.

Deputy Commissioner Bhiwani inaugurated Pilot IT Training Center at Loharu Tehsil on 27th November 2006. DIO Bhiwani delivered the Inaugural Lecture for trainees at the Center.
