## Dr. Neeta Verma, DG, NIC was awarded with ‘Elets Awards of Digital Excellence’

Dr. Neeta Verma, Director General, NIC was awarded ‘Elets Awards of Digital Excellence’ under the category ‘Digital Governance’ for providing Digital Governance during COVID-19 Pandemic, at the 10th Elets Knowledge Exchange Summit & Awards organised by Elets Technomedia.
