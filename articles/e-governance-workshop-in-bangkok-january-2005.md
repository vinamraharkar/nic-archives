## e-Governance Workshop in Bangkok

The regional workshop on ‘Implementing e-Government’ was held in Bangkok, jointly organized by Asian Development Bank Institute (ADBI) and the United Nations Economic and Social Commission for Asia and the Pacific (UNESCA&P). About 50 government officials and experts for the Asian countries attended the workshop and shared their views. The presentation made by Sh. M Moni, DDG, NIC HQ focused on the Government of India’s digital initiatives and agenda for small and marginalized farmers. The prominent projects discussed during the talk were AGMARKNET and AGRISNET.
