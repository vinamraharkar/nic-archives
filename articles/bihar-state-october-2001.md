## Bihar State

The Official web site of Bihar State was recently launched on the Internet by Smt Rabri Devi, Hon’ble Chief Minister, Bihar. The website contains a plethora of information about the State and its various facets such as Government, Education, Tourism etc. Special emphasis has been given to providing online information about the various popular schemes run by the Government for the downtrodden and economically backward citizens of the State. The inauguration function of the website was also attended by Dr N. Vijayaditya, Director General, NIC through Video Conferencing from New Delhi. Dr Vijayaditya spoke on the occasion and emphasized the importance and efficacy of a website in the present IT scenario. The website has been designed and hosted by the Bihar State Unit of NIC. During the ceremony , the Chief Minister of the State also presented a memento to Dr Saurabh Gupta, SIO Bihar as a token of appreciation for NIC’s efforts in developing the website.
