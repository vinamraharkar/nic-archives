## Training for CIC Operators in J&K

The training programme for operators of CIC Blocks covered in phase-I of the project in J&K State concluded recently under which the training for operators of Jammu division was conducted at Jammu and for Kashmir division at Srinagar. Secretary (IT), J&K and Deputy Commissioner Srinagar also delivered lectures on various activities to be performed for making the CICs more effective so that the local community is benefited most.
