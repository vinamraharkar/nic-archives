## AGMARK Exhibition at Orissa

NIC actively participated in the AGMARK exhibition organized at the IDCO Exhibition ground at Bhabaneswar during February 17th –21st, 2004. The AGMARK Stall of NIC was inaugurated by Dr. Bhagirathi Senapati, Vice Chancellor, Orissa University of Agriculture and Technology. He was given a detailed demonstration of the Agmarknet Portal and its utility to the farmer community in the Country. The VC appreciated the pioneering efforts of NIC and hoped that it will go a long way in helping the farmers in the Country.
