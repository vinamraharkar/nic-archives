## Inauguration of Projects over VC, Jharkhand

Presiding over the 4th State Day function, Hon’ble Chief Minister of Jharkhand Shri Arjun Munda inaugurated 5 projects over Videoconferencing. The projects inaugurated are Water Treatment Plant, Sewer Treatment Plant, High Tech Dairy, Sidhu Kanhu Park at Ranchi and Modern Fishery Laboratory at Dhurwa. NIC Jharkhand State Unit provided the Videoconferencing support by connecting all the 5 locations over ISDN Network. Hon’ble CM addressed the authorities present at each site.
