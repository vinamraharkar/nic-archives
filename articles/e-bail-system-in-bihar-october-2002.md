## E-Bail System in Bihar

For the first time in India, the system for transmitting Bail Orders through Email have been started in the State of Bihar. The system has been set-up by the NIC Bihar State Unit in collaboration with the Courts Informatics Division at NIC HQ. Earlier, the bail orders (approximately 450 each day) used to be sent from the High Court to the respective District Courts through ordinary post. This used to cause a lot of delay and sometimes the orders were tampered with. In the new system, elaborate security features have been sent and the bail orders are sent via email, first from the High Court to the various District Courts and then from the District Courts to the litigants.
