## WORKSHOP HELD

Rajasthan: A two-day workshop on Network and Internet was conducted by the NIC Tonk District Unit at the Central Sheep and Wool Research Institute, Avikanagar, Tonk, on September 17 and 18, 1997. Around 120 scientists participated in the workshop. The objective of the workshop was to improve utilization of computers and computer networks in the Institute.
