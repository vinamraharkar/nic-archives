## Website and live webcast of Nehru Trophy Boat Race, Kerala

Nehru Trophy Boat Race is an internationally acclaimed festival event of Kerala that has been taking place for the last 52 years. NIC Kerala launched the first official website of the event, which was inaugurated recently by Sh. K C Venugopal, Hon’ble Minister for Tourism and Devaswom, Kerala. NIC Web Services Division, New Delhi also made a live webcast of the event. The Teleinformatics Development Promotion Programme Division supported providing live feed to the DD sports. The webcast is now available as video-on-demand on the website http://nehrutrophy.nic.in. The webcast received hits from over 18 countries across the world.
