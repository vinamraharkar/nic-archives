## Work Flow Automation System of OSSOPCA receives National eGovernance Award (Silver) at 19th National Conference on eGovernance, Nagpur, Maharashtra

Award: National eGovernance Award 2016 (Silver)
Categ: Innovative use of ICT by State Government/ PSUs
State: Odisha
OSSOPCA Work Flow Automation System improves the pro-ductivity of seeds compared to the manual process in all the four seasons continuously. The complete workflow has seven major steps, which entirely covers the automation through a holistic approach to solve many decade long challenges.
