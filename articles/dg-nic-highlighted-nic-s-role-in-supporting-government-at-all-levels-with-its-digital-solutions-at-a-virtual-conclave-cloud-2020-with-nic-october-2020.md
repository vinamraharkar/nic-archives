## DG, NIC highlighted NIC’s role in supporting Government at all levels with its digital solutions, at a virtual conclave ‘Cloud 2020 with NIC’

Dr. Neeta Verma, Director General, NIC highlighted NIC’s role in supporting the Government at all levels across India, with its digital solutions like eOffice, Aarogya Setu App, DBT, RT-PCR, Video Conferencing and Cloud Infrastructure, during the Covid-19 pandemic. DG NIC, was a keynote speaker at the virtual conclave ‘Cloud 2020 with NIC’, on 24th September 2020 organised by Governance Now.
