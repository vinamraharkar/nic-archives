## NICTRAN IMPLEMENTED

Palamau: The NIC District Unit of Palamau has developed a software, NICTRAN, for the Transport Department, Government of Bihar. The software which has already been implemented has almost doubled the revenue earnings of the Transport Department. The software takes care of tax collection, detection of tax defaulters and false permits, issue of demand notices and reminders, and maintenance of cash books.
