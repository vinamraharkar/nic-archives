## India's Premier Consultant Database

This website gives an overview of the Indian Consultants/ Domain Experts and Consulting Organisations in the ever expanding Indian industrial and infrastructure scenario. The site comprises an Industry Specific Sectoral National Online Database of Consultants and Consultancy Organizations. The portal is designed with an objective to generate business opportunities for Indian Consultants and Consultancy organisations in the domestic and international markets. As a customer, one can access vital information in terms of promoting and advertising their profile as per their specific needs through online registration. The National Database portal covers wide spectrum of sectors such as Agriculture and Rural Development, Banking and Finance, Construction, Education, Energy, Environment, Governance and Administration, Geology and Mining, Health and Demography, Insurance, Information Technology, Law and Regulation, Life Sciences, Manufacturing, Management, Science and Technology, Telecommunication, Tourism, Transport, Urban Development and Water Management.
