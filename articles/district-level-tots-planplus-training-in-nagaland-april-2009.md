## District Level (ToTs) Planplus Training in Nagaland

As the year 2008 being declared as the Year of Capacity building, training at various levels are being imparted. Initially with three districts Mon, Tuensang and Wokha identified for the project, later on with the bifurcation of Tuensang district into two new districts - ie Longleng and Kiphire districts, five districts are being given intensive training. The PLANPLUS Software developed for the online monitoring of BRGF projects was trained to the participants (District level ToT's) from 27-29th October 2008 who will act as the District level Trainers. Ms.Swedesenuo Natso, Sc.Officer & BRGF Project Co-ordinator, NIC and Sh. Apao Mathew Rhezhu, SSA, NIC, Nagaland State Centre were the resource persons. Earlier a three days workshop for State level officers and sensitization for the Elected members (Ministers & MLA's of the five districts) was conducted from 7-9 July 2008 where Mrs. Manie Khaneja and Sh. A.C Gupta, from NIC New Delhi were the Resource persons.
