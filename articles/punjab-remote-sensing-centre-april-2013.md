## Punjab Remote Sensing Centre

Punjab Remote Sensing Centre is an autonomous organization that functions under the aegis of the Department of Agriculture, Government of Punjab. The key role of the organization is to undertake, promote, co-ordinate, aid and guide research and development in the area of remote sensing in Punjab. It carries out important research related work in the field of Geographic Information System GIS), Global Positioning System (GPS) and Remote Sensing (RS).
The website is well organized and diligently designed with quick navigational menus. There is a judicious use of colors. The header image predominantly displays the logo of PRSC. The important services/ solutions provided by PRSC are depicted in the form of banners which run through the image slider on the homepage.
The website offers content related to objectives and Organizational structure of PRSC, services and solutions provided by PRSC, notifications and important projects both ongoing and undertaken by PRSC. The website also provides the important Research Papers published by PRSC in the state.
The website is also provided with a search button to ensure quick access to information. Links to other important websites along with contact information are also provided on the homepage. The website is quite compatible with all major browsers.
