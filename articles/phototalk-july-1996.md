## PHOTOTALK

The inaugural function of NICNET connectivity at Madras High Court. The function was graced by Justice A M Ahmadi, the Chief Justice of India, Justice Ranganath Misra, Chairman, Human Rights Commission, Justice K A Swamy, the Chief Justice of Madras High Court and other Judges of the Supreme court and Madras High Court.
