## Professional Electronic Employment Exchange, Kerala

The Website for Professional Electronic Employment Exchange was inaugurated by Shri Babu Divakaran, Hon. Minister for Labour, Kerala. It is a single window for most of the employment portals and contains the data of about 60,000 professionally qualified job seekers. The site, developed by NIC Kerala State Unit contains a list of all employment exchanges and other employment portals. Employers and their requirements can be viewed on the site and the candidates can also apply online.
