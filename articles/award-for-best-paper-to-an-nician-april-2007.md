## Award for ‘Best Paper’ to an NICian

A Technical Paper on ‘e-Land Record Information System in North Sikkim written by Sh L.P Sharma, NIC Sikkim State Centre was awarded ‘Best paper’ in the 4th International Conference on EGovernance held at IIT Delhi, under the theme technology in government. The paper addresses the challenges faced by the State in the implementation of computerized land record system and describes in detail how the skepticism among the officers was dealt with, and how the system development hurdles were faced, to commence the process, that left no room for any quarter to look back from the system.
