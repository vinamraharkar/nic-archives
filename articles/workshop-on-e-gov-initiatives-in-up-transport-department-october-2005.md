## Workshop on ‘E-Gov Initiatives in UP Transport Department’

NIC UP State Unit conducted a one-day workshop for Assistant Regional Transport Officers (ARTOs) at UP Transport Commissioner’s Office, Lucknow on 5th of September. Sh. C B Paliwal, Transport Commissioner (UP), who was present in the meeting, lauded the efforts for computerization of UP Transport Department, which is being done with the help of NIC. In the meeting a presentation on the on-going computerization project was given to the Transport Commissioner and ARTOs discussed various issues/problems related to the computerization project.
