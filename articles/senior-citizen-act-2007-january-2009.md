## Senior Citizen Act, 2007

Old age is a boon not a curse to humanity! It seeks respect and care as it has brought us humans from the Stone Age to the space age. The spotlight renders all the necessary Information for the maintenance, welfare and protection of senior citizens and parents so as to provide them a secured life of dignity, peace and security. Get to know all about facilities and welfare measures undertaken by the Government for its esteemed senior citizens under Senior Citizen Act, 2007.
