## Spotlight: Online Judicial Services

The Spotlight underscores the various services pertaining to the Judicial System in the Country that been made available online. One can now browse through the websites of Indian Courts and search through the exhaustive databases of all information related to the Indian Courts online. These services include full text of Court Judgements, Daily Orders, Case Status, Causelists and the various Acts of Indian Parliament.
