## Training Programme in Lower Dibang Valley, Arunachal

NIC Lower Dibang Valley District, Roing, in Arunachal pradesh recently conducted a 15 day “Orientation Training Programme on Computers” for the Officers of District Administration and its allied departments. Senior officers including Additional Deputy Commissioner, Circle Officers, Assistant Controller(Legal Metrology), Asstt. Registrar Cooperative Society and Asstt. Mineral Development Officer participated in the training programme. The training was coordinated by Sh. Gyati Doley, DIO.
