## ASHA Soft

State: Rajasthan
Award/s Received: elets-eHealth Healthcare Leaders’ Award
Description: ASHA Soft developed by NIC Rajasthan, is a system for performance measurements and online payment of incentives to ASHAs (Accredited Social Health Activist) across the state in their bank account. The system has been developed for Medical Health & Family Welfare department, Government of Rajasthan and has been operationalized from PHC (Primary Health Centre) level.
