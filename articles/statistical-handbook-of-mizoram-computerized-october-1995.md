## Statistical Handbook of Mizoram Computerized

The NIC Mizoram State Unit has successfully completed the Computerization of the Statistical Handbook of Mizoram. The project was undertaken by NIC for the Directorate of Economics and Statistics Department of the Government of Mizoram. NIC has developed and implemented an appropriate software for the purpose.

The Handbook consists of 135 tables and a total 280 pages. The printed copies of the Handbook were formally handed over to the Directorate of Economics and Statistics, Government of Mizoram, after which it was published by the Directorate. More than 4,000 copies have already been printed by the Directorate and distributed to the offices concerned.
