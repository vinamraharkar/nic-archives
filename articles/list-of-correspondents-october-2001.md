## List of correspondents

Crucial to the success of any group venture is the team spirit of the people involved. The same holds true for Informatics which can fulfill its goal of providing the readers with useful information about NIC’s activities for facilitation of E-Governance only with the co-operation and efforts of the “Correspondents” based in every nook and corner of the Country. With the new look, Informatics has also refreshed its list of correspondents and one/two officers have been nominated from each State/Union Territory who will ensure that their State/UT gets adequate coverage in Informatics. These correspondents will be the contact points for NIC Officials who want to contribute for Informatics. All reports, features and articles for publication are to be sent to the correspondents who in turn will pass them on to the Editorial Team. In case of any major event being organized at any place, the correspondents may be requested to cover the event personally and report the same for publication. A list of the correspondents from various States/ UTs is given below.

Let us all work together and lift Informatics to new heights…….
