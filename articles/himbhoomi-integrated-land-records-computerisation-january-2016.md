## HimBhoomi-Integrated Land Records Computerisation

received CSI Nihilent e-Governance Award 2015
Award: CSI Nihilent e-Governance Award
Categ: e-Governance Initiative
State: Himachal Pradesh
The HimBhoomi is an integrated solution for Himachal Pradesh related to land records, covering registration, land records, maps, lease cases, delivery of Jamabandi at Panchayat level through Citizen Service Centres (LokMitra). It has been a massive exercise over the last 20 years. Started as a true replica of existing Record of Rights (RoR), it got integrated with the Registration process and thereafter, a number of Process Changes (reforms) have been affected in the recent years.
