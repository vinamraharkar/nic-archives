## High Court, Uttaranchal

The Hon’ble Governor of Uttaranchal Sh. Sudershan Agrawal during his recent visit to Nainital inaugurated the official web site of High Court of Uttaranchal in the presence of Hon’ble Chief Justice of Uttaranchal Mr.Ashok A.Desai and other dignitaries. The website has been developed and hosted by NIC Uttaranchal State Unit. Apart from the web site, the Hon’ble Governor also reviewed the working of newly developed software developed by NIC for the High Court computerisation. Sh. Prafulla C. Pant (H.J.S) Registrar General, High Court of Uttaranchal appreciated the work of NIC team led by Sh. Deep Kumar, Officer-in-charge of the Project.
