## Food Cooperation of India (FCI), West Bengal

This site has been developed with an objective to provide credible information about the services on the various Food policy issues forwarded by FCI, WB, to the farmers. The site will help Farmers to keep themselves update on the every changing scenario of the food market related to improvement of systems, processes and resources and at the same time a slew of significant information can be accessed regionally and organisationally. With the latest news on the states tenders, schemes and procurements, the site is sure worth a visit.
