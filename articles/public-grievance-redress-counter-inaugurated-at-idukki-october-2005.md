## Public Grievance Redress Counter Inaugurated at Idukki

The newly constructed Public Grievance Redress Counter at Idukki has been inaugurated recently by Sh. Roshi Augustin, Hon’ble MLA of Idukki. The Counter is equipped with a Computer and peripherals with LAN/Internet connection. NIC Idukki District Unit extends full software support for the counter.
When a person files a grievance petition at the counter, a computer-generated receipt is issued to the petitioner showing the details including a petition registration number. The petition is then forwarded to the concerned officer or department. Once a reply is received from the concerned department, the same is fed in the computer and a computer-generated reply form is sent to the petitioner by post. All the letters are generated in Malayalam.
