## EXPO-INDIA RELEASED

Delhi: NIC has released EXPO-INDIA, a CD containing profiles of 1,18,000 companies belonging to various export promotion councils and major trade bodies. The CD is a valuable source of information for importers and exporters alike. EXPO-INDIA comes with an in-built windows-based query module for details based on specific parameters and their combinations.
