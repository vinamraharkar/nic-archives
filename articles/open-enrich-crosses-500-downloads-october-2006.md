## OPEN eNRICH crosses 500 downloads

The Community Software Solution Framework (OPEN eNRICH Version [OE] 4.0 crossed 500 valid downloads on the 28th of July this year. Open eNRICH has been downloaded in 59 countries to use in rural telecentres/kiosk for content management, portal generation, collaboration and Knowledge Management.
