## DevInfoIndia MODULE AT LBSNAA, MUSSOORIE

Training on DevInfoIndia Module was organized for IAS Professional Course Phase - I at Lal Bahadur Shastri National Academy of Administration, Mussoorie in April 2006. The module was conducted by NIC Training Unit, Mussoorie in collaboration with UNICEF, New Delhi.
DevInfoIndia is a database system which contains social indicators, time periods and geographic areas organized to monitor global and national commitments to sustained human development. The sessions on the module were a Lecture-cum-Demonstration followed by Hands-on. The contents of the sessions included the representation of the data in Tabular Form, Graphical Form, Map Formation etc.
The members of the faculty who conducted the module were Sh. Raj Gautam Mitra, Project Officer, UNICEF, New Delhi, Sh. M. Chakraborty, Sh. Azad Singh & Sh. Amarjeet Singh Dutt of NIC Training Unit, LBSNAA.
