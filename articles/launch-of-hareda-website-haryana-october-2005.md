## Launch of HAREDA website, Haryana

Hon’ble Chief Minister of Haryana, Sh. Bhupinder Singh Hooda launched the website of Haryana Renewable Energy Development Agency (HAREDA) on the eve of Rajiv Gandhi Akshay Urja Divas at Gurgaon on 20th August, 2005. The website has been designed and developed by NIC Haryana State Centre. It provides detailed information on various Non-Conventional Energy Programmes being run by HAREDA, Energy Conservation and Village Electrification Programme beside giving details about various devices being promoted by the department. The website also cover details on various incentives being given by the department to panchayats etc.
