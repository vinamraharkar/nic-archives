## Training for Government Officials, Mizoram

Dr Lalzama, Hon’ble Minister for Higher & Technical Education, Mizoram inaugurated the 5-day training programme on “Utilisation of IT Tools in Government” at the Media Centre at Aizwal on 9th August 2004. The training was sponsored by Development of North Eastern Region (DoNER) and conducted by NIC, Mizoram with the logistic support from ATI, Government of Mizoram.The Hon’ble Minister stressed the need of the hour to effectively use various IT Tools for the socio-economic development of the state. He also thanked NIC for organising such a programme at Aizwal.
