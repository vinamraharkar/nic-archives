## Workshop on Computerisation of Land Records, Alappuzha

A workshop on Computerisation of Land Records was recently conducted at the Collectorate, Allappuzha, Kerala. Nearly 170 officers and staff of the district participated in the programme. The District Collector inaugurated the workshop and delivered a speech in which he stressed the need for computerisation of land records and appreciated the efforts taken by the NIC officers in Alappuzha.
