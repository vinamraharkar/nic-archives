## MCIT Library Consortium

Title: MCIT Library Consortium Authors: PK Upadhyay & Sushant K Panigrahi, NIC HQ Publication: Symposium on Consortia Approach to Resource Sharing- Issues and Policies Abstract: Demand for latest information and impact of ICT in Library & Information Services has compelled every organization to form a consortium of libraries. In India and abroad many consortium initiatives are running successfully. Information professionals have to be aware of different media in which resources are available and to choose the best according to their requirements. This paper was presented at the symposium organized by Documentation Research & Training Centre, ISI in collaboration with INDEST, IIT Delhi and United States Education Foundation in India (USEFI).
