## Dear Editor,

I have been a regular reader of Informatics Bulletin and appreciate the coverage of the e-governance activities from around the Country. Through this medium, I would like to inform your readers that we have set up a District Computer Centre Society at Purnia District in Bihar under my Chairmanship to function as a social mission for imparting training in computer education and Information Technology related activities to the school students of the district. The District Informatics Officer,NIC Purnia has been designated as Secretary of the society which shall be playing a significant role in achieving the goals and objectives of e-governance in the District.
