## NIC Personnel honoured at CSI

The Lucknow Chapter of the Computer Society of India (CSI) recently won the "National Best Chapter Award" for the tenure which had Dr Rakesh Goel and Sh Vinod Taneja of NIC as the Chairman and the Secretary respectively. The officers were honoured at the inauguration function of CSI's Northern Region conference by Sh Vishnukant Shastri, Hon'b'e Governor of Uttar Pradesh.
