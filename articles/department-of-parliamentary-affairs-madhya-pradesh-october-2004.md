## Department of Parliamentary Affairs, Madhya Pradesh

The official web site of Department of Parliamentary Affairs, Madhya Pradesh was recently launched on the net. The contents of the entire web site are in Hindi. The web site provides detailed information on Activities, Rules & Regulations, Orders & Circulars, Budget, Facilities to the MLAs, etc., pertaining to the department.
