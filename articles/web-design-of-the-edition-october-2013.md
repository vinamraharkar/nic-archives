## Web Design of the Edition

http://www.nic.in/

CONTENT
  l Homepage strongly establishes the ownership and purpose of the website.
  l Website is rich in relevant content with back-end to update/ receive contributions from multiple sources.
  l Well-planned information architecture for maximum accessibility.
  l High textual clarity and legibility; light colour background ensures proper contrast for reading along with font enlargement features
  l GIGW compliant website
  l Proper colour coding for links and font handling for content hierarchy
  l Elaborate Accesibility Statement, Terms of Use and Hyperlinking Policy given

DESIGN
  l Well architectured website for usability and easy navigation
  l Simple, minimalistic design yet colour rich homepage and highlighted graphics
  l Consistent and refined usage of fonts, colour scheme and graphical representations
  l Easy identifiable, relevant graphic icons and symbols used
  l Vivid and attractive banners depicting the various projects, products & services
  l Search feature provided to enable quick and relevant search results

TECHNOLOGY
  l Open Source technology used for development and implementation
  l jQuery and CSS have been effectively used for better user experience on home and other pages.
  l Sliding box drupal functionality used for Services, Awards, Documents and Projects sections on homepage.
  l jQuery Carousel plugin used for “Spotlight Slider”
  l Tabber Drupal functionality deployed to enable users to view listings in multiple displays eg. Projects, State wise/District wise search etc.

VERDICT
The new avatar of the website of NIC comes high in terms of looks and usability. The website carries a professional look and feel with a stand-alone identity. It ranks high in terms of content, design and technology used, thus qualifying for the “Web Design of the Issue”.
