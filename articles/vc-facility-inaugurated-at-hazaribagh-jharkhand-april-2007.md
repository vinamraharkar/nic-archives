## VC facility inaugurated at Hazaribagh, Jharkhand

Hon’ble Justice of Jharkhand High Court Sh. Amreshwar Sahay inaugurated the newly installed Video Conferencing Facility between District Civil Court and Lok Nayak Jai Prakash Narayan Central Jail, Hazaribag on 24th February.
He talked with Sh S.P. Kushwaha, I.A.S, I.G. Prisons, who was present in the Jail video conferencing site. This facility does away with the need to produce the prisoners physically in the court for day-to-day deliberations. The efforts of NIC were appreciated on this occasion.
