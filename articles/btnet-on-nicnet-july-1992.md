## BTNET ON NICNET

The growing importance of bioinformatics in the various disciplines of biotechnology led the Department of Biotechnology, Government of India, to establish a Biotechnology Information System network (BTNET). The National Informatics Centre has provided the computer communication facility to BTNET through its satellite-based computer communication network, NICNET.

BTNET is a closed-user group of NICNET and links nine Distributed Information Centres (DICs) and 16 sub-Distributed Information Centres (sub-DICs) with the apex centre known as the Biotechnology Information Centre (BTIC). Micro earth stations are installed at all the DICs. The sub-DICs are to be hooked up to the network through dial-up modems.
