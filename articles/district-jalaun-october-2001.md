## District Jalaun

Jalaun District in Uttar Pradesh recently launched its website on NIC’s web server. The website provides useful information about the various on-going projects in the District and also gives a link to important statistics. Also present on the website is the complete Telephone Directory of Government Departments in the District. The website, designed and developed by NIC Jalaun District Unit was formally launched by the District Collector Sh. Chandrabhanu, who appreciated NIC’s efforts in establishing the presence of Jalaun on the World Wide Web.
