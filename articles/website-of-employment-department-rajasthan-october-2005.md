## Website of Employment Department, Rajasthan

Website of the Employment Department of Rajasthan was inaugurated recently by Dr. Kirodi lal Meena, Hon’ble Minister for Food & Civil Supplies, Disaster Management (Rajasthan). Through this website jobseekers will get latest information concerned with employment and can also obtain information relating to fortnightly Employment News, Registration Forms, Circulars of the Department, Nagrik Adhikar Patra, Rojgar Melas, List of Registered Placement Agencies etc. The website also contains messages from the Chief Minister, Cabinet Ministers and other State Ministers.
