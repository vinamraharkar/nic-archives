## New Website Launch: District Muktsar, Punjab

Website of District Muktsar (Punjab) has recently been inaugurated by Hon'ble CM, Punjab on the auspicious occasion of Maghi at Muktsar. The occasion was graced with the presence of Deputy CM, Punjab and Minister of Power, Punjab. DC, Muktsar appreciated the efforts of NIC for making the project successful. Hon'ble CM also had a Video Conferencing session with District Patiala.
