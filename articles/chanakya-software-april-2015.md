## Chanakya Software

State: Bihar
Award/s Received: State E-Governance Excellence Award at eBihar Summit, “Excellence in Govt. Process Re-Engineering”
Description: Chanakya is a role based solution for automation of university business process related to Registration and Examination Modules. Registration Module covers the registration of students in the university and it includes Registration Form Data Entry or capturing of students details from OMR sheet of Registration Form through OMR/OCR device.
