## Computerisation at Regional Workshop on Fiscal and Governance Reforms

A top level regional workshop on fiscal and governance reforms and poverty reduction strategy was held at Berhampur in Orissa on 15th & 16th July, 2002. NIC District Unit, Ganjam was entrusted by the district administration to provide IT Support to the workshop. In the workshop five groups were formed to discuss on five specific topics, to give their valuable suggestions to the Government. During the discussison the data was entered online and the final reports were generated. The District Administration awarded merit certificates to the NIC team on the successful accomplishment of the workshop.
