## Hon'ble Union Minister of State Dr. Jitendra Singh, Inaugurated NIC’s eOffice Product Suite at National Agri-Food Biotechnology Institute (NABI) and Centre of Innovative & Applied Bioprocessing (CIAB) at Mohali, Punjab

Hon'ble Union Minister of State (Independent Charge) Science & Technology and Earth Sciences; MoS PMO, PP/ DoPT, Atomic Energy & Space, inaugurated NIC's eOffice product suite at National Agri-Food Biotechnology Institute (NABI) and Center of Innovative & Applied Bioprocessing (CIAB) at Mohali, Punjab. In a major step towards efficacious and transparent decision making and seamless electronic movement of organization files in National Agri-Food Biotechnology Institute (NABI) & CIAB, Dr. Jitendra Singh, Hon’ble Minister inaugurated eOffice facility on Tuesday 2nd November 2021. This is the key initiative taken by NABI and CIAB towards empowering digital institutional activities under the mission ‘Digital India’
