## BRAIN-STORMING SESSION

New Delhi, DEC: The newly formed Co-operative Informatics Division of the National Informatics Centre will conduct a one-day brain-storming session on NICNET Utilization for Co-operative Sector on January 19, 1994 at the India International Centre, Max Mueller Marg, New Delhi.

Central and State Government officials of the Co-operative Department, representatives from NABARD, NCUI, NCDC, etc. will participate in the session.
