## Inauguration of Job Portal at Himachal Pradesh

Hon’ble Chief Minister HP Sh. Virbhadra Singh dedicated the Job Portal of the Labour and Employment Department to the employers and job seekers in the State. The credit for developing the software for the Job Portal goes to NIC Himachal Pradesh State Centre. This unique technique opened the doors for vibrant opportunities for the educated unemployed youths of the state as they can now apply directly to their jobs through the Job Portal on time. The process of compulsory notification under the Employment Exchange (CNV) Act has thus been made web enabled.
The CM lauded the efforts of the National Informatics Centre and HP Information Technology Department in bringing about IT revolution in a large number of departments and organizations. He honored the officers and IT professionals from the National Informatics Centres, HP Labour & Employment Department and HP IT Department, who have been involved in the mammoth task of system study, process re-engineering, software development, hardware procurement & installation, training, data entry and implementation.
