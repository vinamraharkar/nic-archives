## Chief Justice of India avails NIC’s VC facilities

The Hon’ble Chief Justice of India Sh. K.G. Balakrishnan who was on a visit to Kochi recently availed NIC’s Video Conferencing facility at Lakshadweep Administration office, Willingdon Island, Kochi for interacting with the Hon. Judges at National Judicial Academy, Bhopal. During his 20-minute interaction, he thanked NIC and Lakshadweep office for providing the VC facilities in the island. Earlier he was received by Sri. A.K. Wasnik, IAS, Secretary (Information Technology), Lakshadweep, Sh. B.Amanulla, Hon. District Judge, Lakshadweep. Sh. Ajith Brahmanandan, SIO, Lakshadweep and Sh. A.Balakrishnan, Secretary to the Hon. Administrator, Lakshadweep.
