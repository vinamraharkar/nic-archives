## Editor's Note

This issue finds its way in your hands when the various Units of NIC are having a breather after a hectic yet extremely satisfying and successful activity of publishing the Exam Results on the Internet. In one of the sections, we have tried to capture the spirit behind this mammoth annual exercise from a human angle and see how e-governance is reaching out and touching peoples’ lives in more and more ways. A special feature in this issue is also on “Elitex-2003” which saw an active participation from NIC and proved to be a platform to showcase the tireless efforts of our various offices in pursuit of effective electronic governance.

Happy Reading…..
