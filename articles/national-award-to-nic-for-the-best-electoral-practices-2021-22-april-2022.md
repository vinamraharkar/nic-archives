## National Award to NIC for the Best Electoral Practices 2021-22

NIC Puducherry Union Territory Unit received an award under ‘Special Category’ for their technical contributions to General Elections 2021 at the National Awards for the Best Electrol Practices 2021-22, which were held in New Delhi on the occasion of the 12th National Voters Day. The Chief Secretary, Puducherry and Chief Electoral Officer, Puducherry bestowed this Award on the NIC Puducherry Centre and appreciated the NIC efforts.
