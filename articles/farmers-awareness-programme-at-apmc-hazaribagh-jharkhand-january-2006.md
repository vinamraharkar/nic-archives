## Farmers’ Awareness Programme at APMC Hazaribagh, Jharkhand

Recently, a Farmers’ Awareness Programme was organised at APMC Hazaribagh, Jharkhand. It was jointly organised by DMI, Ranchi and NIC in coordination with APMC,Hazaribagh. More than fifty farmers from different villages of the district participated in the programme. On this occasion, Sh. N L Singh, SMO, DMI, Ranchi highlighted the benefits of Agmarknet portal (http://agmarknet.nic.in). Sh. P K Patel, Scientist-C, NIC demonstrated to the farmers how to use the Agmarknet portal for knowing the market prices of various agricultural produce. Queries of the farmers regarding the Agmarknet project were also answered. Sh. S K Deo, DIO, Hazaribagh assisted in conducting the programme.
