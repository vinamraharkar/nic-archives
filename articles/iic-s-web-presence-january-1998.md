## IIC's WEB PRESENCE

Delhi: NIC has developed and hosted a web site for the Indian Investment Centre (IIC), a Government organization for investment promotion. With information on India's investment climate, economic policies and the Union Budget, along with useful contact addresses, the site is expected to draw potential investors. The IIC website can be accessed at http://www.nic.in/iic/
