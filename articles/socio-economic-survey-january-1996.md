## Socio-economic Survey

The NIC Unit at Kuralagam State Government Office Complex Madras, has processed data relating to the socio-economic survey on minorities, for the Department of Evaluation and Applied Research, Government of Tamil Nadu.
The processing involved generation of more than 700 tables and statements which were used by the Department to report its findings on the socio-economic condition of religious minorities in the State.
The survey covered the Buddhist, Christian, Jain, Muslim, Parsee and Sikh communities.
