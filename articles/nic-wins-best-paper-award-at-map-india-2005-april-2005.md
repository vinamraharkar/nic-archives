## NIC Wins 'Best Paper' Award at Map India 2005

At the recently held Map India 2005, Mrs. Mahalakhmi Narayanan presented a paper titled 'Web GIS for Census of Tamil Nadu using Open Source'. The paper won an award under the 'Best Paper' category.
