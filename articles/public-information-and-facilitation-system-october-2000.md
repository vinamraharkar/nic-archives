## Public Information and Facilitation System

A Public Information & Facilitation System [PIFS] has been developed by NIC-Haryana to facilitate the public to get the information about various Schemes of Haryana Government. Public can have information about any scheme, its eligibility conditions, procedure, contact addresses, forms required to get the benefits of the scheme and much more. PIFS is GUI based generalized ready-to-use software and available in front-end and back-end modules. The database of sectors, departments, schemes and their details is created using back-end module. Front-end module is again available in two modes viz. Stand alone and Web-enabled. The same database, created back-end module, is used for both the front-end modes. The data can be maintained in any of the formats.
