## NIC Officers elected on CSI-Lucknow Board

Owing to their valuable contributions in the field of Information Technology for the State of Uttar Pradesh, three officers from NIC U.P. State Unit, Lucknow have been elected as office bearers of Computer Society of India, Lucknow Chapter for the year 2003-2004. Dr. L.R. Yadav, Technical Director, NIC has been unanimously elected as Chairman, while Mohd. Amir Idrees has been elected as Secretary and Mr. Deepak Sharma as Treasurer.
