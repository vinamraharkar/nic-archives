## Elets Innovation Award for Digidhan Dashboard

NIC’s Digidhan Dashboard has received the Elets Innovation Award 2022 under the category for “Digital Governance”. The award function was part of the Elets AatmaNirbhar Bharat Summit, which is a platform to celebrate the spirit of innovation and recognize path breaking initiatives taken in the field of Information and Communication Technology. The two day summit was held during 19th-20th April 2022 in New Delhi.
The Digidhan dashboard acts as the primary platform for the government to track growth of digital payments. It automatically consolidates the data from Reserve Bank of India (RBI), National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI), 110 public sector/ private sector / payments / regional / rural / foreign banks and uses Business Intelligence (BI) reporting to generate assorted reports.
