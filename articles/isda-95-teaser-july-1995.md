## ISDA-95 (Teaser)

New Delhi: A two-day Conference on Informatics for Sustainable Agricultural Development, 1995 (ISDA-95) was organized by NIC in collaboration with the Ministry of Agriculture, Ministry of Rural Areas and Employment and Department of Fertilizers on May 24 & 25, 1995, at the Vigyan Bhawan, New Delhi.
