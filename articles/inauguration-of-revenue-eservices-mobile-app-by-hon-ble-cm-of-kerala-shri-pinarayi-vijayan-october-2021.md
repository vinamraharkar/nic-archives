## Inauguration of ‘Revenue eServices & Mobile App’ by Hon'ble CM of Kerala, Shri Pinarayi Vijayan

Hon’ble Chief Minister of Kerala, Shri Pinarayi Vijayan, inaugurated various Revenue eServices, revamped Revenue Land Information System (ReLIS) Portal, Revenue ePayment Portal, and Mobile Application for Revenue eServices on 9th September 2021 in the presence of Shri K. Rajan, Hon’ble Minister of Revenue, Shri Antony Raju, Hon’ble Minister of Transport, Shri G R Anil, Hon’ble Minister of Food & Civil Supplies, Shri P Prasad, Hon’ble Minister of Agriculture, Shri Ahammad Devarkovil, Hon'ble Minister of Ports, Shri K. Krishnankutty, Hon’ble Minister of Electricity (through VC), Team NIC (through VC), Shri Biju K, Land Revenue Commissioner, Secretaries, and Officials from Revenue Department.
Revenue eServices Mobile App are designed and developed by NIC Palakkad District Centre for the Land Revenue Department, Government of Kerala.
