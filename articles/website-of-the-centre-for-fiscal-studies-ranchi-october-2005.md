## Website of the Centre for Fiscal Studies, Ranchi

The Centre for Fiscal Studies (CFS), Ranchi has gone online with the launch of its website on 16th August by Mr. George Deikun, Mission Director, USAID, India. Sh. Raghubar Das, Hon’ble Finance Minister, Jharkhand was the Chief Guest at the occasion. Sh. Rahul Sarin, Secretary Finance, Jharkhand and Sh. P P Sharma, Chief Secretary and Chairman, Governing Body, CFS were present on the occasion along with officials of various government departments, USAID team, and media representatives. The website has been designed and developed by NIC, Jharkhand State Centre.
