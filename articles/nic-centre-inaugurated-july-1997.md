## NIC CENTRE INAUGURATED

Bhubaneshwar: The Chief Secretary, Shri R K Bhujabal, Government of Orissa, recently inaugurated the NIC Orissa State Secretariat Centre inside the Orissa State Secretariat premises at Bhubaneshwar. Besides providing consultancy, data entry and software development jobs; the Centre will organize regular training programmes for the employees of the State Government at different levels.
