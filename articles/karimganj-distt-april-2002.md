## Karimganj Distt

Karimganj Distt http://karimganj.nic.in From Ajit Bhattacharyya, NIC Assam

Karimganj District of Assam was amongst the first few districts of Assam to have taken the initiative to launch their website. The site has been designed and developed by the NIC Karimganj District Unit and presents comprehensive information about the Profile, Demography, History, Communication, Administration, Economy etc.
