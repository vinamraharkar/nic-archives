## ‘Yada Kada Stambha’ at NIC HQ

The Hindi section at NIC HQ has launched a special column named ‘Yada Kada Stambha’ on the notice board having standardized Hindi equivalents that are used in noting/drafting and correspondence in Hindi. The motive is to propagate Hindi with some special maxims pointed out by reputed celebrities and national leaders in favour of our official and national language Hindi.
