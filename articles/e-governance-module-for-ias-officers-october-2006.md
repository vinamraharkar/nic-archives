## E-Governance Module for IAS Officers

An E-Governance Module was recently organized during IAS Professional Course Phase I (2005 Batch) at Lal Bahadur Shastri National Academy of Administration, Mussoorie The topics covered a lot of key aspects related to E-Governance Frameworks and Applications. The module was conducted jointly by NIC HQ, NIC Uttaranchal State Unit and NIC Training Unit, Mussoorie.
