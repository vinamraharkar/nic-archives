## Workshops

- On GISTNIC, organized by NIC Western Regional Centre, Pune on November 21, 1992. GIST databases were explained with simultaneous on-line demonstration. Discussions with potential Users also held.
- On Computerization of Technical Education Department, organized by NIC Varanasi District Unit in June, 1992, to involve the Technical Education Staff in the computerization effort in the Department. Views of the participants were solicited for incorporation in the system.
