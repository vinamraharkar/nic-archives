## LECTURE ON NIC ACTIVITIES

**Rajasthan: The National Informatics Centre**, organized a lecture-cum-demonstration on NIC activities on January, 29, 1996, at the NIC Rajasthan State Unit, Jaipur. The Chief Secretary, Government of Rajasthan presided over the programme.

The programme was attended by the Secretaries from Finance Department, Planning, Rural Development and Directorate, Rajasthan Institute of Public Administration.
