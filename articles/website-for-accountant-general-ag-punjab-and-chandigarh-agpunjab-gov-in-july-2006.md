## Website for Accountant General (AG) Punjab and Chandigarh (agpunjab.gov.in)

Sh. Pawan Kumar Bansal, Hon’ble Union Minister of State for Finance inaugurated the website for the office of Accountant General (AG) Punjab and Chandigarh on 31st March, 2006. The function was held in the office chambers of Shri. Nand Lal, AG Punjab and UT Chandigarh.
The site has been developed by NIC, Punjab State Centre, Chandigarh. In addition to the basic information about the department, its organizational setup, responsibilities, forms download the site also offers the status of pensioners’ cases submitted to the AG office and model guidelines for preparing the pension cases.
