## Una & Chamba Districts, H.P.

Introduction to some of the new websites launched on NIC’s servers in the past quarter…..
Una & Chamba Districts, H.P.
http://hpuna.nic.in,
http://hpchamba.nic.in
From Ajay Chahal, Himachal Pradesh Correspondent
The Una and Chamba districts of Himachal Pradesh went online with the formal launch of their official websites by Prof. Prem Kumar Dhumal, Hon’ble Chief Minister of Himachal Pradesh, in two separate functions held at the respective district headquarters. The websites cover history, facts at a glance, tourism, access, maps, fairs, administration, IT, citizen interfaces, downloadable forms, searchable telephone directories and photo galleries containing beautiful photographs of natural scenery and pilgrimage points. The websites have been designed and developed by NIC District Centres, Una and Chamba.
