## Land Records Computerization, District Una

On Una Diwas, Hon’ble Chief Minster Shri Virbhadra Singh inaugurated & dedicated computerized land records to the people of Tehsil Bangana, District Una. Deputy Commissioner, Una Sh Rajneesh Kumar, IAS gave a brief presentation, explaining the operations of HimBhoomi software and efforts put in the computerization of land records data and making it online. In his speech, the Hon’ble CM congratulated the district administration and distributed certificates of appreciation to officials for their contribution in the projects. He also praised the efforts of NIC, in helping the state government in achieving its targets of computerization. Hon’ble CM presented a computerized Jamabandi to the Deputy Commissioner and also distributed Nakals to the people of Tehsil. He also inaugurated the E-Vikas project for DRDA Una developed by NIC District Centre, Una.
