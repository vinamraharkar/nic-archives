## COMPUTERIZED LAND RECORDS

Sikkim: Sikkim is going to be the first state in our Country to successfully complete the mammoth task of computerizing the land records. The basic data entry work is over in all the four districts of Sikkim. This will be followed by subsequent updations in the land records called 'Mutation', in each district. Till date more than six lakhs records of land holding documents 'Khasara' and 'Khatiyan' have been entered. The ultimate objective is to make the system on-line.
