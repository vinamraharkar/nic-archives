## LG's OFFICE COMPUTERIZED

New Delhi: NIC has been entrusted with the responsibility of computerizing the office of the Lt Governor of Delhi, Mr Tajendra Khanna.
Presently, a Software System developed for Public Grievances Monitoring and Files Monitoring is operational and the UNIPLEX Software has been installed. Some new software systems are under development. Necessary hardware has been made available.
