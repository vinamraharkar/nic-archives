## Inauguration of Bihar State Data Centre

Hon'ble Chief Minister of Bihar Sh. Nitish Kumar recently inaugurated the Bihar State Data Centre established by NIC. The inauguration function was presided over by Dr. Shakeel Ahmad, Hon'ble Union Minister of State, Ministry of Communications & IT and Dr. B. K. Gairola, Director General, NIC; New Delhi participated as a special guest. In his address to the audience, Dr. Gairola spoke about the technology behind the Data Centre & the pivotal role it will play. Dr. Saurabh Gupta, STD & SIO, Bihar gave an elaborate presentation on NIC's Project Profile for the state.
