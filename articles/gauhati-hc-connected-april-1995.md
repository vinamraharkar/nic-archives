## GAUHATI HC CONNECTED

Guwahati: The Chief Justice of the Supreme Court of India, Justice AM Ahmadi inaugurated the new NIC-Gauhati High Court Computer Centre at the Gauhati High Court on February 5, 1995. The day also marked the inclusion of the Gauhati High Court in the network of high courts connected by NICNET under the COURTNIC project of NIC.
