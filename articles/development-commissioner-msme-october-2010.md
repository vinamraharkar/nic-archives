## Development Commissioner (MSME)

This bilingual website of Development Commissioner (MSME)-functioning under MSME, Govt. of India; is intended to serve as a comprehensive guide to public in general and prospective entrepreneurs in particular. The mission of the website is to impart superior vitality and growth impetus to the Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME) in terms of output, employment and exports and instilling a competitive culture based on heightened technology awareness. The website has updat- ed user friendly data& statistics, annual reports, details of financial, legal and technical aspects of MSME along with the MSME cluster development reports. The Publication section contains valuable resources like the Committee reports, circulars, Trade Reports and Books and Periodicals released by the Ministry from time to time on Indian SSI sector. Endeavor has been made to make available updated, correct and accurate information.
