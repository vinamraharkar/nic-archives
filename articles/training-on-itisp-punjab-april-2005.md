## Training on iTISP, Punjab

Integrated Treasuries Information System Punjab (iTSIP) has been implemented at district level treasuries. Now, with the technical support of DIOs and infrastructural support from Department of Treasuries & Accounts of Punjab, the project has been extended to sub-treasuries level in Punjab. In order to spread awareness and address the issues related to project expansion, training of district treasury officers was organized at Chandigarh. The training was also attended by Director, Treasuries & Accounts and several state Level officials.
