## business.gov.in- Your online Business guide now in Hindi

Besides being the national language in India, Hindi is the largest non-English language used in India. The release of the Business section of “India.gov.in” portal in Hindi, offers all the features in our National Language with more flexibility, transparency and data control. Get a crystal-clear picture of performance in all customer-facing areas viz Starting Business, Doing Business Abroad, Investment Opportunities, Indian Economy, Taxation etc. at http://business.gov.in/hindi/default.php
