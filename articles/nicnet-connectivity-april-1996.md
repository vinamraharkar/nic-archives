## NICNET CONNECTIVITY

**Mizoram**: The NIC Mizoram State Unit has provided NICNET connectivity to the Animal Husbandry and Veterinary Directorate of the Government of Mizoram, enabling them to have a national hook-up. The department is now able to make use of the electronic system to submit progress reports to the Central Government directly.
