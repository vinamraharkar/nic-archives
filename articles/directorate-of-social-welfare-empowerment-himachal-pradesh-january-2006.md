## Directorate of Social Welfare & Empowerment, Himachal Pradesh

Directorate of Social Welfare & Empowerment, Himachal Pradesh: Sh. Rangila Ram Rao, Hon’ble Minister for Social Welfare & Empowerment launched the website for the department of Social Welfare & Empowerment recently. On this occasion Sh. Bhim Sen, Principal Secretary, Social Justice & Empowerment and Smt. Anuradha Thakur, Director, Social Justice & Empowerment were also present. This is the first Hindi website in Himachal Pradesh. It provides information related to various schemes and projects of the department, Integrated Child Development Scheme, NGOs etc.
