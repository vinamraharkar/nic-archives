## e-Procurement

State: Kerala
Award/s Received: eINDIA Awards 2014
Description: Government eProcurement software (GePNIC) application developed by National Informatics Centre, caters to the process of online tender publishing, bid submission, bid opening and award of contract, to help bring in transparency in the tendering process. The software is web enabled and built on industry standard software technologies and best practices.
