## eVidhan

State: Himachal Pradesh
Award/s Received: Himachal Pradesh State Civil Services Award – 2015
Description: eVidhan has enabled the HP Assembly to become the first digital Assembly in the country. Shri Brij Bihari Lal Butail, the Honourable Speaker of HP Assembly has been the driving force in getting it implemented. Under eVidhan, touch screens have been installed on the seats of all the members of the State Assembly and complete proceedings, questions, answers and support documents are made available to the members in eBook format.
