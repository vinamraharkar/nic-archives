## District Governance Mobile Challenge Awards Virtual Ceremony

District Governance Mobile Challenge (DGMC) was launched in January 2021 for NIC District Centres with the objective to motivate DIOs to embrace mobile first approach in District Administration. 674 districts across the country took part and 374 mobile apps were developed under the challenge.

Dr. Neeta Verma, Director General NIC conferred the District Governance Mobile Challenge Awards in the virtual ceremony held on 28 May 2021.

Best Mobile app
Gold: Nammude Kozhikode, Kozhikode District, Kerala
Silver: My Gram Panchayat, Hisar District, Haryana
Bronze: m-Nivaran, Faridkot District, Punjab
Bronze: ePermissions, Shimla District, Himachal Pradesh
