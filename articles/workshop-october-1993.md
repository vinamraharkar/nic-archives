## WORKSHOP

A ONE-DAY WORKSHOP ON DISNIC GEOMINE was jointly organized by the Eastern Regional Centre of the National Informatics Centre, NIC(ER), Bhubaneshwar and the Directorate of Mining and Geology of the Government of Orissa on July 31, 1993 at NIC(ER).

Officers of different functional backgrounds such as Geoscientists, Mining Administrators, Information Technology Professionals and Statistical Officers participated in the workshop to deliberate on the theme of Computerization of Mineral Information in Orissa.
