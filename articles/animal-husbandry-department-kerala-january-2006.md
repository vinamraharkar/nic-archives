## Animal Husbandry Department, Kerala

Animal Husbandry Department, Kerala: Website of the Department of Animal Husbandry, was inaugurated recently by the Animal Husbandry Commissioner in a function presided by Sh. Subrata Biswas, Secretary, Govt. of Kerala (Animal Husbandry & Diary Development). Dr. S K Bandyopadhyay, Animal Husbandry Commissioner, Government of India, Dr. B Ashok, Director of Animal Husbandry, Dr. S R Jayakumar, Joint Director, Southern Regional Disease Diagnostic laboratory, Bangalore, Dr. K. Prabhudas, Project Director, Project Directorate on Animal Disease Monitoring and Surveillance, Bangalore, Sh. T Mohana Das, TD, NIC Kerala and other eminent persons attended the function.
