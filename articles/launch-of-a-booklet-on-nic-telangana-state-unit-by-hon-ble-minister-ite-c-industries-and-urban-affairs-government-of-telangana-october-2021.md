## Launch of a booklet on NIC Telangana State Unit by Hon'ble Minister ITE&C, Industries, and Urban Affairs, Government of Telangana

Shri KT Rama Rao, Hon'ble Cabinet Minister for IT E&C, MA&UD and Industries & Commerce Departments, Government of Telangana has launched the Booklet on NIC Telangana State Unit on 8th September 2021 in the presence of Shri Jayesh Ranjan, Principal Secretary of the Industries & Commerce (I&C), Information Technology (IT) Departments Shri K. Rajasekhar, DDG & State Informatics Officer & NIC Officers.
