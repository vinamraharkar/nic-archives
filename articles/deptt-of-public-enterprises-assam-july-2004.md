## Deptt of Public Enterprises, Assam

The website of the Department of Public Enterprises, Government of Assam was inaugurated by the Chief Secretary of Assam at a recent function held at Dispur. The website, developed by Department of Public Enterprises, and designed and hosted by NIC, Assam State Centre, contains information on all state level public enterprises, statistical data and other related information of various public sector bodies.
