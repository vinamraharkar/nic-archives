## Director General (NIC) Felicitated

At a function organised recently by the Bhoovigyan Vikas Foundation, Dr. N. Vijayaditya, Director General (NIC) was honoured, among other dignitaries. DG (NIC) was presented a memento by Sh. Sompal, Member, Planning Commission. The IT Luminaries from across the Country had gathered at the occasion where a Lifetime Achievement Award was presented to Dr. N.R. Narayana Murthy, Chairman & Chief Mentor, Infosys in recognition of his contribution towards India’s global pre-eminence in the area of Information Technology. The award was presented by Sh. T.N Chaturvedi, Hon’ble Governor of Karnataka and Dr. K V Sundaram, Chairman Bhoovigyan Vikas Foundation.
