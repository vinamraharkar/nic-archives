## Web Portal on AIDS Awareness, Haryana

With an objective to spread awareness about AIDS among masses through all possible means of communication, Haryana State AIDS Control Society has launched a web portal on AIDS awareness, designed and developed by NIC Haryana, to incorporate Internet services for the purpose. Hon’ble Health Minister of Haryana Dr. ML Ranga inaugurated the portal on December 1st, World AIDS Day and appreciated the efforts put in by NIC Haryana in enhancing the quality of health services through computerization.
