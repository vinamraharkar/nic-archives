## Sachivalaya Gatepass System

Uttar Pradesh Sachivalaya, spread across several prominent Govt buildings and housing a large number of ministers and senior Govt officials is one of the largest in the country. All the buildings have multiple gates for entry and managing the visitors is a Herculean task. Sachivalaya Gatepass System which was started on a trial basis, has proved to be a huge success in its pilot phase. Huge commotion at the gates has given way to hassle-free monitoring of movement of visitors now. Sachivalaya administration maintains a visitors' database now. They can keep a record of details such as time/purpose of their visit. The process is on for its replication at all the gates of the Campus.
