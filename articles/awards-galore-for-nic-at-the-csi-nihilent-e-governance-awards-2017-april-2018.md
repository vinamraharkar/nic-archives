## Awards Galore for NIC at the CSI-Nihilent e-Governance Awards, 2017

Awards Galore for NIC at the CSI-Nihilent e-Governance Awards, 2017
The Computer Society of India’s Nihilent e-Governance (CNeG) Awards have paralleled the evolution of National e-Governance plan [NeGP], Digital India and Digital Transformation initiatives of the Country. These awards were instituted way back in the year 2002. The 2017 award ceremony, which was held in the historic city of Kolkata and as part of the 52nd Annual Convention on 20th January, 2018. NIC has excelled by winning several awards in various categories. Following include the Awards list.
