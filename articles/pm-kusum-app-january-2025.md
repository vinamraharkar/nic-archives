## PM-KUSUM App

The Ministry of New and Renewable Energy (MNRE), Government of India has introduced the Pradhan Mantri Kisan Urja Suraksha evam Utthan Mahabhiyan (PM-KUSUM) Scheme to support farmers in adopting renewable energy for agricultural purposes.
The PM-KUSUM mobile app is a vital tool designed for farmers, State Implementing Agencies (SIAs), and empanelled agencies to streamline the scheme's benefits and ensure efficient management.
Through this app, farmers can monitor the functioning of their solar-powered pumps in real-time, ensuring optimal performance and reducing downtime.
Additionally, the app provides a platform for farmers to log complaints related to pump operations and track their status, fostering transparency and timely resolution of issues.
By integrating renewable energy with digital solutions, the PM-KUSUM app not only enhances the efficiency of agricultural operations but also contributes to sustainable energy practices, empowering farmers to adopt eco-friendly methods while addressing their energy needs effectively.
