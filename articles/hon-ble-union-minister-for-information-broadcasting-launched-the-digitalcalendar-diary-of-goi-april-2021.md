## Hon'ble Union Minister for Information & Broadcasting launched the DigitalCalendar & Diary of GoI

Hon’ble Union Minister for Information and Broadcasting Shri Prakash Javadekar launched the Digital Calendar and Diary of Government of India. In a ceremony held at National Media Center, Shri Javadekar, with the click of a button unveiled the Android and iOS mobile applications of the calendar and diary. It was developed in collaboration with NIC by Bureau of Outreach & Communication, Ministry of Information & Broadcasting, Government of India. This App has the provision for scheduling meetings, taking notes securely in digital form, lists official holidays and important dates and provides information on schemes, events & publications of the Govt.
