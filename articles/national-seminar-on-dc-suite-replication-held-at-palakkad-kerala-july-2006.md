## National Seminar on DC*Suite Replication held at Palakkad, Kerala

National Seminar on DC*Suite Replication was held at Palakkad in May 2006. The Seminar was inaugurated by Shri P.H.Kurien IAS, Secretary (IT), Govt. of Kerala in the presence of Shri. D. Dutta, Sr. Technical Director, NIC, New Delhi. Shri. V.S. Raghunathan, SIO, NIC Kerala delivered the welcome address & presented the vision of the project while Dr. Mahesh Chandra, MD, NICSI released the multimedia CDROM on the project.
Representatives from NIC State Units & District Centres from various parts of the country participated in the seminar which included DC*Suite product demo, technical presentations, replication strategy and panel discussion on Replication.
The delegates were given a live demonstration of the actual workflow at the collectorate, Palakkad, which has undergone a modernization exercise with infrastructure facilities like Data Centre, Rack Servers, Touch Screen Kiosks, Video Conferencing etc.
