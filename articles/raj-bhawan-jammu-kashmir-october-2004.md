## Raj Bhawan, Jammu & Kashmir

J&K Governor Lt Gen. (retd) S K Sinha recently launched the official website of Raj Bhavan. ‘The computerization of the Raj Bhavan Secretariat would bring about more efficiency in the working of its different units’, he said while lauding the efforts of NIC in developing the site. VSAT communication equipment has been installed both at Jammu and Srinagar Raj Bhavan and all the users brought under Local Area Networking. Describing the facility as of great importance, General Sinha hoped that viewers would get detailed information about the functioning of Raj Bhavan and its different facets.
