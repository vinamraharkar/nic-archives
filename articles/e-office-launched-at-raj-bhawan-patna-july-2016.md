## e-Office Launched At Raj Bhawan, Patna

e-Office for Raj Bhavan, Patna was formally inaugurated by His Excellency, the Governor of Bihar, Shri Ramnath Kovind on 19th May 2016. Shri Bala Prasad, Principal Secretary to the Governor, Shri Rajesh Kumar Singh, SIO (Bihar) and other Government officials were present during the inauguration. Inception of e-Office at Raj Bhavan accomplishes the Governor’s desire for streamlining the process and timely movement of files electronically. Earlier, for the implementation of e-Office in Raj Bhawan Secretariat, NIC has submitted a proposal to Principal Secretary to Governor. The pre-requisite formalities accomplished at Governor Office and Master database such as Employee Master Details (EMD), Email IDs/ User IDs of officers and staff, File Header of different sections were created. Capacity building of officers and staff was organized at Raj Bhavan to understand the operational part of the application for smooth implementation. During the occasion, Shri Bijay Kumar, PSA (NIC) demonstrated the e-Office software. His Excellency, the Governor appreciated NIC Officers for the successful implementation of e-Office at Raj Bhavan.
