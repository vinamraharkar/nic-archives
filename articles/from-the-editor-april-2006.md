## From the Editor

We march ahead taking quantum strides bringing accolades and adding feathers to our cap. Our efforts have been greatly appreciated, recognized, and rewarded. Yes, friends it was an award time, several of our projects have been awarded for their effectiveness, service delivery and global standards. I am sure we will continue in the same spirit and strive towards bringing greater glory to our organisation.

In this issue we have featured CIPA (Common Integrated Police Application), a project developed in collaboration with NIC, which will ensure higher levels of transparency in police functioning. This issue also highlights the visit of a high level delegation from the Royal Government of Bhutan to NIC headquarters and Assam, and the finalisation of a broad framework of collaboration for e-Governance application technologies and solutions between the two countries.

As you browse through the pages, I am sure you will be able to analyse the stories, articles and give us your feedback, which will definitely help us to improve.
