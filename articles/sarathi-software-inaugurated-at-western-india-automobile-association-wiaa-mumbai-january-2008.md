## SARATHI Software inaugurated at Western India Automobile Association (WIAA) Mumbai

The Learner's driving licence component of sarathi 2.0 software of NIC was recently inaugurated at WIAA Mumbai by Sh. G. S. Gill, Principal Secretary Transport and State Excise, Government of Maharashtra in presence of Sh.. S. D. Shinde, Transport Commissioner Maharashtra State, and Sh. Moiz Hussian Hussain Ali, SIO, NIC - Maharashtra. Subsequently, PS Transport, Transport commissioner, SIO Maharashtra and WIAA president addressed the gathering. PS acknowledge the efforts taken by NIC for making installation of software in the event. SIO, Maharashtra in his address mentioned the importance of e-governance and commitment of NIC to give support to e-governance projects.
