## Legislative Assembly, Mizoram

Shri Lalchamliana, Hon’ble Speaker, Mizoram inaugurated the official website of Mizoram Legislative Assembly at a special function. The website provides information about the organizational set-up of the Assembly, its historical background, Profile of the Leaders etc. During Inauguration, the Hon’ble Speaker said that assembly proceedings would be webcast in the near future with technical support from NIC.
