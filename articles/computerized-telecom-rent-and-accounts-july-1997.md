## COMPUTERIZED TELECOM RENT AND ACCOUNTS

Panaji: The National Informatics Centre, Goa State Unit has successfully computerized the Telecom Rent and Accounts Section of the Department of Telecommunication, Panaji-Goa. The Computerized System takes care of the following functions of the Telecom Rents and Accounts Section:
* Generation of Trunk Call and Telephone Bills.
* Generation of Public Call Office (PCO) Bills.
* Posting of bills paid and other demand notes.
* Generation of bills, register and sub-ledger.
