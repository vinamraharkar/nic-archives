## Hon'ble PM acknowledges NIC Services at Raj Bhavan, Trivandrum

During the recent 3-day visit of the Hon'ble Prime Minister of India to Trivandrum, complete ICT support was provided to the Prime Minister's Camp Office at Raj Bhavan. The Prime Minister appreciated the efforts of the NIC Kerala team posted at the Raj Bhawan for coordinating these activities.
