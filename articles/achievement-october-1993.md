## ACHIEVEMENT

Mr SUBBU THANGAVIL, working as a Systems Analyst in the Health Informatics Division of NIC, was recently selected by the Centre of International Co-operation for Computerization (CICC), Japan, to undergo a CICC-sponsored two-month specialized course on UNIX-LAN System Design and Development.

Mr Thangavel was one of the only two Indians selected for this much-coveted scholarship through a worldwide competitive examination held by CICC, Japan.
