## ‘Seva Ratna’ Title for Sh.M.Moni, DDG NIC

Deputy Director General Sh.M.Moni was conferred with the title ‘Seva Ratna’ by the Centenarian Trust recently for his contribution in extending the applications of Information and Communication Technology to agriculture and rural development in India. The award was given by the Swamiji Sri Jayendra Saraswathi Avl at a function held at Chennai.
