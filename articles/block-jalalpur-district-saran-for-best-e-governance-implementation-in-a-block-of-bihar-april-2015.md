## Block Jalalpur District Saran for best e-governance implementation in a Block of Bihar

State: Bihar
Award/s Received: State E-Governance Excellence Award at eBihar Summit, “Award for best e-governance implementation in a Block of Bihar”
Description: Block Jalalpur, District Saran is heralding the ICT revolution by implementing ICT initiatives such as digitization of the database of State and Central Pensioners, generation of Bar coded Passbook of all block pensioners, tracking of Pensions distribution at block and Panchayat level, etc.
