## New Budget Head Schema in Rajasthan Treasuries Computerization

A new 15-digit budget head schema has been successfully implemented in all the treasuries and sub-treasuries of the Rajasthan in place of earlier 18-digit schema. This change has been initiated by NIC as desired by Finance Department, Rajasthan for the sake of uniformity across the country. A 2 day state level exhaustive workshop was also conducted to make the treasury personnel acquainted with the new schema.
