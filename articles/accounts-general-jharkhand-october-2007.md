## Accounts General, Jharkhand

This site aims at focusing on the accountability of the executive to the Parliament and State Legislatures by carrying out audits in the public sector and providing accounting services in the states in accordance to the Constitution of India and Laws as well as best international practices. On browsing this informative site, one can learn about the latest audit reports, disclosures under right to information act, locate retirement benefits and gather information about various tender notices declared by the AG office, Jharkhand. The website provides vital information and data on all audit accounts of the State Government and one can easily analyse the performance of various departments.
