## Food & Supplies and Consumer Affairs, Chandigarh Administration

The site designed and developed by NIC, Chandigarh is a vital corner for accessing varied information related to food & supplies and consumer affairs under Chandigarh Administration. The objective behind this newly launched website is to protect consumers from exploitations by making them aware of their consumer rights. It is a vehicle for enabling people to secure speedy and inexpensive redressal of their grievances. Apart from this, it also provides useful information on Public Distribution System (PDS), various Acts and statistics along with particulars of the administration's functions and duties. Various forms and applications related to ration card distribution can now be easily downloaded from this website. Overall, the launch of the website is a boon to improve the department's service on a broader scale.
