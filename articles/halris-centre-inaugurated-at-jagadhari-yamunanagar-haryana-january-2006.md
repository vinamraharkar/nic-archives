## HALRIS Centre inaugurated at Jagadhari (Yamunanagar), Haryana

Capt. Ajay Singh Yadav, Hon’ble Revenue Minister, Haryana inaugurated Nakal Services (copies of Records-Of-Right) at Haryana Land Records Information System (HALRIS) Centre at Jagadhari Tehsil in Yamunanagar District on 27th October, 2005. HALRIS is an integrated software developed by NIC Haryana State Centre for management of all activities related to Land Records like Jamabandi, Khasra Girdavari and Nakal etc. It has also been linked with the Haryana Registration System (HARIS). Deputy Commissioner, Smt. Sukreti Lekhi, during her speech, highlighted the salient features of the system. Speaking on the occasion, Hon’ble Revenue Minister said that the new system would bring transparency and save time, energy and unnecessary harassment of the farmers who used to visit Patwaris for innumerable times to get their work done. Anyone owning property at the Tehsil can see updated information of his land holding through various queries by using touch screen kiosk established at the centre and get updated nakal of jamabandi.
