## Computerized Provident Fund Office, Purnea

Computerized Provident Fund Office at Purnea district was recently inaugurated by the District Magistrate of Purnea, Sh. Pankaj Kumar. Speaking on the occasion the DM said that with computerization of PF Office the works can be done speedily and retired govt. officials will get retirement benefits timely. The software generates the Annual GPF Statement/ Accounts through the data entered by the Treasury officers/staffs of District Treasuries from Purnea, Araria, Kishanganj and Buxar against the individual GPF subscriptions. The GPF software ‘Bhawishyanidhi’ installed at Purnea District PF Office has been designed and developed by NIC Bihar State Centre, Patna. Hon’ble DM also praised the sincere efforts of Shri GK Mishra, DIO, NIC District Centre, Purnea.
