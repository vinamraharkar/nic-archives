## CHILD LABOUR JHARKHAND (http://childlabourjharkhand.nic.in)

This website has been launched by the Government of Jharkhand, to ensure healthy, educated & empowered children and to tackle the problem of child labour. The aim of this website is to carry out vulnerability mapping on a priority basis to identify the areas and trades which employ maximum children.
The content rich and visually appealing website has been built with an aim to provide maximum accessibility and usability to its visitors by facilitating quick access to various schemes like ICPS, ICDS, MGNREGA, NCLP, Mid Day Meal etc. The website is continually updated with e-bulletin, videos and information, press releases and policy updates pertaining to the target beneficiary group. Detailed information on the Child Labour (Prohibition & regulation Act, 1986) and related laws is also available.
The design is attractive with use of pulsating colours. Site wide navigational menu on the top provides quick and consistent navigation. Four content boxes serve featured section in a striking manner. The right bottom block on the home links to national portal and other important sites. The website is elegant in presentation and layout.
The website contains a FAQ section and a feedback page to assist user seeking information. The contact page enlists email address and contact number of the important officials. A font adjustment option at the header provides better reading experience.
