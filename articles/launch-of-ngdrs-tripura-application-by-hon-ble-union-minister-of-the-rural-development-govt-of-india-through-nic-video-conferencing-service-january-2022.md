## Launch of NGDRS-Tripura Application by Hon'ble Union Minister of the Rural Development, Govt. of India through NIC Video Conferencing Service

Launching of National Generic Document Registration System (NGDRS) in Sub Registry Offices (SRO) of West Tripura & Sepahijala Districts by Shri Giriraj Singh, Hon’ble Minister of Rural Development & Panchayeti Raj, Government of India, on 3rd December 2021 in presence of Shri Fagan Singh Kulaste, Hon’ble MOS, RD and Steel, Government of India. Shri Biplab Kumar Deb, Hon’ble Chief Minister of Tripura, Shri Narendra Chandra Debbarma, Hon’ble Minister of Revenue & Forest of Tripura, Shri Kumar Alok, IAS, Chief Secretary of Tripura, Shri Hukum Singh Meena, Addl. Secretary, MoRD, DoLR, GOI, Smti. Tanusree Deb Barma, Secretary, Revenue Department, Tripura, Shri C. K. Dhar, DDG & SIO, NIC Tripura, Shri A. M. Joshi, Scientist-F, HOD NGDRS Pune and Shri Rajiv Goel, HOD NIC, DoLR, Delhi was also present in this inaugural function along with other officials and citizens from various SRO Offices and Districts of Tripura.
