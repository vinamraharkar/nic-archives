## Idukki Distt

Idukki Distt http://idukki.nic.in From George Eapen, NIC Kerala

The Official Website of the Idukki District of Kerala was launched by the Hon’ble Revenue & Law Minister of Kerala Shri K.M.Mani at a special function. The site has been designed and developed by the Idukki District Unit of NIC and presents comprehensive information about the Profile, History, Infrastructure, Administration, Economy, Tourism, Information Technology etc.
