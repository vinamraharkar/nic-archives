## Computerization in Cuttack Municipal Corporation

With the implementation of computerized Birth & Death Registration System and Holding Tax Collection Monitoring System by NIC Orissa State Unit, the Cuttack Municipal Corporation became the first corporation in Orissa to go for automation. The Tax Collection Monitoring System facilitates the creation of database of assessees, calculation of different taxes, generation of tax collection register and various important reports. The Birth and Death computerization system concentrates on issue of certificates, monitoring birth, still birth and death records and generating reports. The system was inaugurated by Shri Ashirbad Behara, Chairman, Cuttack Municipal Corporation.
