## Editorial

Civilizations have thrived by adopting and preserving a tradition wherein experts advise to choose appropriately from available paths for growth and progress. This tradition called Counseling serves useful purposes in many areas, such as medicine, legal, parenting, sports, etc, and has found an especially useful application in academics to help students in choosing the courses where they may stand better chances at faring well.
E-Counseling is NIC’s effort to ensure that the time-tested tradition of Counseling is sustained with the help of technology to overcome challenges posed by unexpected situations such as natural disasters and pandemics. Technology-enabled E-Counseling allows Counselors and students to connect virtually so that appropriate choices can be made and the right courses can be assigned to aspiring students.
