## Energy Billing System, Tripura

Hon’ble Chief Minister of Tripura, Shri Manik Shankar Sarkar launched the computerized Energy Billing System for Electrical Division I of Agartala Municipal Council. The project is a collaborative effort of Power Department and NIC, which is a workflow based solution for Electricity Boards to deal with financial data and energy auditing. A web enabled touch screen kiosk has also been deployed for customer assistance and to bring transparency to the system.
