## HimKosh-Integrated Finance Management System

bags CSI Nihilent e-Governance Award 2015
Award: CSI Nihilent e-Governance Award
Categ: e-Governance Initiative
State: Himachal Pradesh
HimKosh provides an online integrated Financial Management System by inter-linking receipt and expenditure data generated at treasuries and release of allocated fund by finance department to their field functionaries with the basic objective of improving Financial Reporting System.
