## NIC wins at the Open Group India Awards 2022

NIC conferred ‘Award of Distinction in Digital Innovation’ under the TOGAF® Standard / Enterprise Architecture Category by The Open Group, for creating a successful and well thought out solution National Generic Document Registration System (NGDRS) covering the entire lifecycle—from inception to outcomes, at the Open Group India Awards 2022.
NGDRS is an end-to-end workflow enabled document registration system which allows citizens across 15 States and UTs of India to access the state website and submit their documents for registration at the respective sub- registrar office online. As of now, over 35.5 million registrations have been done using NGDRS application. The prestigious award was received by Shri I.P.S. Sethi, DDG, on behalf of NIC.
