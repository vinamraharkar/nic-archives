## NIC OFFICIAL AWARDED

Pune: Ms P P Joag, Senior Technical Director, NIC, was conferred with the 1997 award for the Woman making outstanding Contribution to the IT industry in Pune instituted by the Pune Chapter of the Computer Society of India (CSI), on October 3, 1997.
