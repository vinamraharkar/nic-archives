## TRAINING FOR COURTS

Gujarat: The NIC Gujarat State Unit has recently conducted a training programme during July 1 - 5, 1996 for the employees of eight district courts of Ahmedabad (rural), Ahmedabad (city civil), Bharuch, Sundernagar, Surat, Baroda, Kheda and Mehsana. NIC, Gujarat also has plans for conducting training programme for the remaining eleven district courts by October this year.
