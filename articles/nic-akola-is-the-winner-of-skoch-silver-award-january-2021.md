## NIC, Akola is the Winner of SKOCH Silver Award

NIC, Akola is the Winner of SKOCH “ORDER OF MERIT” and SKOCH SilverAward of Technology in SKOCH Awards 2020, in the 68th SKOCH Summit virtually held on 30th Nov. 2020, with the theme “State of Digital Governance” for the initiative “Framework for e-Governance of fundamental resources Electricity, Water”
