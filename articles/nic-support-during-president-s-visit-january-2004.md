## NIC Support during President’s Visit

During the visit of Hon’ble President of India Dr. A.P.J Abdul Kalam on July 6th, 2003 to Mumbai, the NIC State Centre Mumbai provided the necessary Computer and Internet connectivity to the President and his entourage. ICT facilities were provided at the Camp office of President at Sahyadiri Guest House of Govt. of Maharashtra.
