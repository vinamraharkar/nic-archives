## Launch of Web based Merit cum Means Scholarship System for Ministry of Minority Affairs

HON'BLE Minister of Minority Affairs, Sh. Salman Khurshid launched the 'Student Module' of the Online Scholarship Management System (OSMS) for Merit-cum-Means based scholarship scheme of the Ministry of Minority Affairs recently. Sh. Vivek Mehrotra, Secretary, Ministry of Minority Affairs, and other Senior Officers of the Ministry were present on the occasion. Using this facility the students can apply for Scholarship online from anywhere, anytime.
A web based system has been designed and developed by NIC-Tamilnadu State Centre as part of the e-Scholarship project in coordination with NIC HQ. The site can be accessed through URL http://momascholarship.gov.in . It is a complete work flow system that handles scholarship applications from receipt of online application, pro-cessing at the State level and disbursement of the same to Minority students and it has been developed using Open Source software tools. The system is being implemented across the country.
