e-filing of Return by Dealers for Department of Commercial Taxes,Tamil Nadu
More than 25,000 dealers in Tamil Nadu are filing their VAT monthly returns online using the www.tnvat.gov.in website designed and developed by NIC, Chennai.

VAT was introduced in Tamil Nadu on 1st January 2007. More than 5 Lakh Dealers are registered under VAT. The e-Filing of Returns was launched initially for top most 49 Dealers during September 2007. Later, this facility was extended to 20000 Dealers in a phased manner. The Top 20000 Dealers cover 95% of the total tax collected from the VAT Dealers. Dealers can file the Returns along with purchase and Sales Annexures. Facilities are available to upload the Annexures of all purchases and sales. Separate acknowledgement is generated for the Returns filed online.

Home Page of tnvat.gov.in

Filing of Return is mandatory for top 20000 Dealers. Facility is also provided to the other Dealers to opt for Online Return filing. So far 8530 Dealers have opted for online filing of Returns in addition to the Top 20,000 mandatory Dealers.
R.Gayatri, Tamil Nadu

Inauguration of Nivrutti Vetan Wahini Portal, GOM, Maharashtra
Nivrutti Vetan Wahini, a portal for Pensioners of Maharashtra was inaugurated by Hon'ble Sh. Jayant Patil, Minister for Finance and Planning, Government of Maharashtra, on Thursday 21st August, 2008 at Mantralaya, Mumbai. The Finance Minster, Sh. Jayant Patil applauded the contribution made by NIC, SDU, Pune in the Computerisation of the Finance Department

The portal which has been developed by NIC, SDU, Pune and the Directorate of Accounts and Treasuries, GOM allows pensioners to interact with treasuries. SMS Seva at RTO, Kanpur, Lucknow and NOIDA
National Informatics Centre UP State Unit, Lucknow has developed mobile based SMS software for customising for UP Transport Department. The software provides a mobile based service directly to citizens/ transporters and transport department officials to get the status of Road Tax and Additional Tax. Just by sending vehicle registration number through SMS at Mobile Numbers 09794988000 for RTO, Kanpur, 09910514897 for ARTO, NOIDA and Sh Ashok Kumar, Deputy Transport Commissioner & Sh Jai Sankar Tiwari, ARTO, Noida inaugurating SMS based services in their respective offices 09450365853 for RTO, Lucknow the sender can get the status of Road Tax and Additional Tax paid by him. The software uses the same database prepared through VAHAN software. The first of this kind of service has been started for the vehicles registered at RTO, Kanpur. This service will be of great help in trapping the Tax Defaulters. The SMS based service was inaugurated by Sh. Ashok Kumar, Deputy Transport Commissioner on 16th July, 2008. The inaugural function was graced by Sh. Nirmal Prasad RTO Kanpur, Sh. Sanjay Tiwari ARTO Kanpur, Dr. Y K Singh Technical Director NIC, Sh. Piyush Srivastava PSA NIC and Sh. S K Bajpayee PSA NIC.
