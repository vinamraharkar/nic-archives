## DG, NIC Visits Jalandhar

Dr. N. Vijayaditya, DG, NIC visited Sant Baba Bhag Singh Institute at Adampur and NIC Jalandhar office in April 2006. He was accompanied by Senior Officials from NIC hqrs & Punjab State Unit. DIOs from Ludhiana, Kapurthala, Hoshiarpur and NIC Officers posted at Passport Office and Registrar of Companies, Jalandhar also attended the meeting.
DIO, Jalandhar & Kapurthala made a presentations on major eGovernance initiatives taken by NIC, especially SUWIDHA, PRISM & newly established Video Conferencing facility between Central Jail & District & Sessions Court at Jalandhar. DG showed keen interest in the services being rendered by District Administration to the Citizens. He was deeply impressed by the implementation of Property Registration System (PRISM) - which has brought down the time taken for property registration to less than 30 minutes.
