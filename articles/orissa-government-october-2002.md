## Orissa Government

The Government of Orissa recently launched its official website on NIC’s web server. The site provides comprehensive information to the interested visitors about all aspects of the State, ranging from demography to Government set-up and from Tourism & famous temples in the State to even local weather. Information on management of calamities like drought, flood and cyclone are special features of the site. The website, designed and developed by NIC Orissa State Unit, was formally inaugurated by Sh Naveen Patnaik, Hon’ble Chief Minister, Orissa at a special function.
