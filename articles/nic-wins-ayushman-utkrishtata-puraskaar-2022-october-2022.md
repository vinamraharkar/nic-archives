## NIC wins Ayushman Utkrishtata Puraskaar 2022

NIC received the ‘Ayushman Utkrishtata Puraskaar 2022’ for being the top government integrator with the highest number of health records linked to the Ayushman Bharat Health Account (ABHA), at Arogya Manthan 2022 organised by National Health Authority (NHA), in New Delhi.
Ayushman Utkrishtata Puraskaar is given in recognition of excellent services provided by the organisation for taking the Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission a step forward.
The award was received by NIC Health Projects Division, which is spearheaded by Shri Sunil Kumar, Dy. Director General, and Dr. Piyush Gupta, Sr. Technical Director.
The NIC Health Projects team includes Smt. Suparna Bhatnagar, Technical Director, Shri Pawan Kumar Yadav, Technical Director, Shri Atul Verma, Scientist-C, Smt. Megha Gupta, Scientist-B, Sushri Vidhi Sethi, Scientist-B, and Shri Tapas Kumar, Scientist-B.
