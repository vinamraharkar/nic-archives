## Vidhan Sabha Info at Finger-Touch

In an e-governance endeavour, NIC-Punjab has deployed touch screen kiosk in the State Vidhan Sabha. The information kiosk was inaugurated by Hon’ble Finance Minister of Punjab Sardar Lal Singh recently. The Kiosk will help the Members of Legislative Assembly to know about the various schemes announced by the Government, Questions of the House, Meetings of the various Committees, Detailed introduction of the members of the house etc.
