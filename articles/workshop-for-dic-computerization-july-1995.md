## WORKSHOP FOR DIC COMPUTERIZATION

New Delhi: A one-day Workshop on Computerization of District Industry Centres, organized by NIC in collaboration with the Department of Small Scale Industries on June 15, 1995, concluded with the signing of MoUs between NIC and State Governments concerned for the computerization of 92 leading District Industry Centres.
