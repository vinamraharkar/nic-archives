## EDUCATION HOMEPAGE

New Delhi : The Homepage for Department of Education on NICNET was inaugurated by the Minister of Human Resource Development, Mr S R Bommai, on March 4, 1997.

The Page can be either accessed from NIC's Homepage (URL: http://www.nic.in) or directly through the following address: http ://www.nic.in/ education

The Page provides information regarding the various Sectors of Education such as School, Higher, Technical, Management, Adult & Vocational Education and Book & Language Promotion. Besides this, other details of the Department such as Set-up & People, Policy Framework, Schemes etc are also given in the Page.
