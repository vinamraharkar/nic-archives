## 2015 Waseda-IAC International e-Government Rankings

Waseda University, Tokyo, in cooperation with the International Academy of CIOs (IAC), has released the results of its annual international e-government rankings survey for 2015. This publication represents the eleventh consecutive year of the global e-government survey by the team of Professor Toshio OBI, Director of the Waseda University Institute of e-Government and experts at worldwide IAC member universities. The results of the survey find Singapore in first place, followed by the United States, Denmark, the United Kingdom and Korea. India’s position is 35th with a score of 56.42.
