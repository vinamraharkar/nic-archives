## COMPUTERS FOR HC

Chandigarh: The Chief Justice of India, Mr JS Verma, inaugurated NIC computer facilities in the Punjab and Haryana High Court at Chandigarh, on November 30, 1997. The computer setup, implemented by NIC Haryana State Unit, span three sites in the High Court, and is linked through a Local Area Network (LAN) based on thick ethernet. Computers will now be available for use by judgement writers and private secretaries of Judges.
