## Storage Networking Summit at Mumbai

The second Storage Networking Summit, a two day international conference organized by the Storage Networkin Industry Association was held at Mumbai during December 3-5, 2003. The Conference was inaugurated by Dr.N.Vijayaditya, Director General, NIC and was attended by over 500 delegates from all over the world. During his inaugural address, Dr. Vijayaditya stressed on the growing importance of the concept of Storage Networking, especially in the Indian context. A team from NIC attended the Summit which also comprised useful talks and tutorials on the relevant issues.
