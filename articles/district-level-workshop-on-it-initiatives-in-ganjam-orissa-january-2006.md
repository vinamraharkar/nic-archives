## District level workshop on IT initiatives in Ganjam, Orissa

A district level workshop on IT initiatives for development works and use of computers, was held at the conference hall of Ganjam collectorate recently under the collaboration of Ganjam IT Society, NIC and UNDP. It was inaugurated by UNDP team leader Sh. P K Jena, IAS and presided by Sh. S.K.Singh, Collector, Ganjam. The workshop was coordinated by Sh. Subash Ch. Misra, DIO, Ganjam. Block Development Officers, CDPOs, all the district level officers and persons from NGOs attended the workshop.
