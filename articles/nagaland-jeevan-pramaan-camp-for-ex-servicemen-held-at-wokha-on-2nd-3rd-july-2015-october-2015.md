## Nagaland: Jeevan Pramaan Camp for ex-servicemen held at Wokha on 2nd & 3rd July 2015

Jeevan Pramaan Camp was held on the 2nd & 3rd July, 2015 at NIC, Wokha District Centre for the ex-servicemen of Wokha District at DC Office Complex, Wokha, Nagaland. This camp was arranged with the Digital India Week celebrations at Wokha District, wherein a total of 144 pensioners were issued the Digital Life Certificates. The event was organized by Rajya Sainik Board, Nagaland, Kohima led by its Director, Brig. K. K. Roy Choudhary, SM, VSM (Retd.). Throughout the camp days, Sh. George Kath Rengma (DIO NIC), Wokha District Centre and his team provided an active technical support. NIC Co-ordinator of Jeevan Pramaan in Nagaland, Sh. Lanusungkum Aier (PSA) and his team members, Ms. Novotsono Natso (SO), were present at Wokha on the first day of the camp and provided necessary technical support for setting up the systems for the successful conduct of the camp.
