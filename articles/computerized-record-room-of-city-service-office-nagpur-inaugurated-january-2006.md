## Computerized Record Room of City Service Office, Nagpur inaugurated

Hon’ble Guardian Minister and Minister of State for Textiles, Sh. Satishbabu Chaturvedi inaugurated the Computerized Record Room of City Survey Office-2, Nagpur. The City Survey Office is using CODISS (Computerised Documents Index Search System) software developed by NIC. During the function, the DIO and DIA of Nagpur were felicitated for their contribution to the project.
