## Directorate of Pension, Assam

The Hon’ble Chief Minister of Assam Shri Tarun Gogoi inaugurated the Directorate of Pension, Government of Assam website at Janata Bhawan on 26th February 2004. The website, developed and designed by NIC Assam State Centre contains information about the latest pension cases resolved, procedures, guidelines and forms related to pension for school teachers, districtwise and PPO-wise query for individual cases. The content of the site will benefit all retired teachers and those who are about to go for superannuation.
