## New Website Launch: Dept of Biotechnology & Bioinformatics Centre, Barkatullah University, Bhopal

The official website of the Department was launched recently. The website has been designed and hosted by NIC MP State Centre. The site is very informative and contains detailed information related to the Department, major thrust areas, courses, facilities, admission procedures, research areas, training programmes etc.
