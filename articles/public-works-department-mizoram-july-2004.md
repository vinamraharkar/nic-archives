## Public Works Department, Mizoram

The website Of Public Works Department, Mizoram was inaugurated by Er. Ramhluna Khiangte, Secretary & Engineer in Chief, Mizoram at a simple function attended by officers and Media persons on 20th May. Speaking on the occasion, Sh Khiangte said that the website is a small but a significant step taken up by PWD, Mizoram along with NIC towards the e-governance . This website contains a plethora of information relevant to other government departments, contractors as well as common citizens.
