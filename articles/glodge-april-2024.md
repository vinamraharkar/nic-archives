## gLodge

The gLodge app offers a convenient solution for individuals seeking to book rooms in govern-ment-operated accommodations, including Circuit Houses, Inspection Bungalows, Guest Houses, and Tourist Lodges across various locations within the East Siang District.
Designed to streamline the booking process, gLodge provides users with a user-friendly inter-face to browse available accommodations, check room availability, and make reservations directly from their mobile devices. Whether for official visits, tourism purposes, or personal trips, the app simpli-fies the task of securing accommodation in govern-ment-operated facilities.
With gLodge, users can access comprehensive information about each accommodation option, in-cluding amenities, room types, and pricing details. This allows travelers to make informed decisions based on their preferences and budget constraints.
By offering an efficient and transparent booking platform, gLodge aims to enhance the overall ex-perience of travelers visiting the East Siang District. Whether for business or leisure, the app provides a hassle-free way to arrange accommodations in gov-ernment-operated facilities, ensuring a comfortable and convenient stay for all visitors.
