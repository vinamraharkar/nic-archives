## Rajiv Internet Village

Rajiv Internet Village/ePanchayat Project was inaugurated by the Hon'ble Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh, Dr. YSR Reddy in Vempadu Village in Vishakhapatnam district of Andhra Pradesh. DIO, Vishakhapatnam Shri N Suresh Kumar demonstrated the Birth & Death Registration software to Dr. Reddy. He appreciated the efforts of NIC and congratulated the District Centre for the implementation of the software.
