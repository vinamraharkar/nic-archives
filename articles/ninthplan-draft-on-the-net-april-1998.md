## NINTHPLAN DRAFT ON THE NET

Delhi : The Ninth Five Year Plan (1997-2002) draft was recently placed on the Internet by NIC for the Planning Commission. Besides the two volumes of the complete draft document running into around 1600 pages, the site also contains the Executive Summary and the observations made by the Deputy Chairman of the Planning Commission.

URL : http://www.nic.in/ninthplan
