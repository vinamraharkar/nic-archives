## Parliamentary Standing Committee on IT review NIC Orissa State Unit, Bhubaneswar

A Parliamentary Standing Committee on Information Technology reviewed the activities and projects of the Orissa State Unit of NIC. Sh S. K. Panda, SIO & Sr. Tech Director, Orissa presented the successful initiatives of NIC Orissa State Center on different projects including Infrastructure, Networking, Video Conferencing, e-Governance, Training, Portal Development etc.
