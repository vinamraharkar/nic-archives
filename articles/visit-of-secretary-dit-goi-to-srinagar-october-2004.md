## Visit of Secretary, DIT, GOI to Srinagar

Sh. K.K. Jaswal, Secretary IT, GOI visited Jammu & Kashmir on 2nd September 2004 during which he visited the new CIC sites at Ganderbal, Leh and Kangan blocks in Srinagar. Hon’ble Secretary exchanged ideas on deriving maximum benefits from the CIC. He remarked, “A welcome initiative. I hope it will affect the life of the people in a significant way.” He held a meeting with the NIC Officers posted at Srinagar and reviewed the progress of CIC project implementation in J&K.
