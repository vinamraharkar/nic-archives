## NIC at Women Scientist Conference

NIC recently participated in a conference cum exhibition on ‘Women Scientists and Technologists : Role in National Development’ held at Vigyan Bhawan, New Delhi. The NIC stall at the exhibition highlighted activities in the areas of Bio-Medical Databases, Bio-Technology Projects, GIS & Remote Sensing, Rural Bazaar, Training, Community Information Centres (CIC) and generated a lot of interest among the visitors who appreciated NIC’s services in these areas.
