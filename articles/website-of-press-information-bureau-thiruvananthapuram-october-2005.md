## Website of Press Information Bureau, Thiruvananthapuram

The website for Press Information Bureau (PIB) Kerala, which is developed by NIC Kerala State Centre was inaugurated recently by Sh. P H Kurian, IT Secretary, Kerala. Sh. Anand Singh, Director IT Mission, Sh. Kesavan, Dy. Principal Information Officer, PIB, staff of PIB and NIC Kerala attended the function. The website is developed using the Open Source Content Management Tool Mambo. The website is in Malayalam and its content is updated by PIB Thiruvanathapuram and Kochin Offices.
