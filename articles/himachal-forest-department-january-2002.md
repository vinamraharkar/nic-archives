## Himachal Forest Department

Prof. Prem Kumar Dhumal, Chief Minister of Himachal Pradesh, inaugurated the website of the HP Forest department
on 1st of October, the Wild Life Day. The website provides useful information about the forest cover in HP, Eco-
Tourism, wildlife, legislation, events, forest maps etc. The directory of Forest officers is also available on the website in
the shape of a database with various search options. The site has been designed and developed by NIC Himachal
Pradesh State Unit, Shimla.
