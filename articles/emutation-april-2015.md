## eMutation

State: Maharashtra
Award/s Received: SKOCH Order of Merit Award 2014
Description: eMutation is regarding the updation of Land Records through various types of Transactions on Land Parcels called as “Mutation”. These mutations are recorded as Registered Documents or Non-Registered Documents. The mutations for Registered Documents are first done at Sub Registrar Office (SRO).
