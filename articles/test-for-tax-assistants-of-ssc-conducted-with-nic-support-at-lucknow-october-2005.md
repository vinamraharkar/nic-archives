## Test for Tax Assistants of SSC conducted with NIC support at Lucknow

On-line Data Entry Speed Test for Tax Assistants of Staff Selection Commission was successfully conducted recently at NIC Kendriya Bhawan, Lucknow. Around 800 candidates appeared in the examination, and Lucknow was the largest centre in the country. Forty Internet-ready computers were installed at the examination site as a special arrangement for the test. The candidates were briefed, trained and provided with all technical assistance during the test.
