## Multipoint VC at Chandigarh

Multipoint Video Conferencing facility at the UT Secretariat, Chandigarh was recently inaugurated by Lt Gen JFR Jacob (Retd), Hon’ble Governor of Punjab and Administrator, UT Chandigarh. The facility has been provided at 12 locations in the Secretariat by NIC using its Local Area Network and the latest technology and software which allow multiple users to participate simultaneously in a VC session from different locations.
