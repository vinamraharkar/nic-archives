## eShramik, the online Application for registering the building and other construction workers of Odisha wins SKOCH Award

Award: SKOCH 2016 Award
Categ.: Best Portal
State: Odisha
eShramik facilitates the various stakeholders for making benefits available to building and other construction workers for education, marriage, unfortunate accidents & deaths and other benefits. The Application also facilitates collection of cess. The system quite useful for Labour Commission Office, District Labour Offices and Establishments who employ labourers. The award was received by Dr R N Behera, Sr. Technical Director and Sri S.K.Sahoo, Scientist-D (NIC, Odisha) at the International Convention Hall, HICC, Hyderabad.
