## Student Internet World 2004 at Bijapur, Karnataka

The Student Internet World 2004 was organised as a part of IT.COM 2004 at Bijapur. The event was collectively organised by District Administration, Zilla Panchayat, Education Dept and DIT. Nearly 8000 students benefited from this event. Training comprised of an introduction to Internet and its usage, followed by practice sessions. The event was supported by different departments and NGOs like KPTCL, RTO, Education Dept, Mahithi Sidhu and Yuva.com. Technical support and supervision was extended by NIC Bijapur. The event received wide publicity and appreciation from the public and students.
