## National Portal Update

“india.gov.in”, the National portal of India provides single window access to information and online services for the citizens and other stakeholders. Recently, the portal added another feather to its cap by inaugurating the Content Service Provider (CSP) enablement for Kerala. As part of the inauguration, Hon'ble CM Sh. V.S. Achuthanandan uploaded the first Kerala content (using CSP Login) to the National Portal Smt. Asha Varma, TD, NIC Kerala State Centre briefed the Kerala activities of the National Portal. Sh. Mohandas V.S., TD, National Portal Team presented the features of National Portal and spoke about the roles and responsibilities of the National Portal Coordinators to the dignitaries gracing the occasion.

Smt. Asha Varma & Sh. Mohan Das V.S. on the occasion.

RTI Complaint & Appeal
The Government of India always lays emphasis on making the lives of its citizens easy, smooth and making India truly democratic. Alternatively, every citizen has this right to know about the government and its role and function. The newly added section RTI Complaint & Appeal in National Portal of India is a valuable attempt in this process and a one stop source for all queries related RTI complaint & Appeal. The section not only provides all the RTI act information but also caters to a slew of ways to access these informations substantially viz., when to file and lodge a complaint, draft a complaint, check complaint status. Besides, one can also access information on when to file and submit an appeal, check second appeal status etc. To enrich your knowledge more on RTI Complaint & Appeal log on to http://rti.india.gov.in/.
