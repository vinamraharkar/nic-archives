## DACNET Training at J&K

A Training Programme for Officers of Central Integrated Pest Management Centre (CIPMC), Jammu/Srinagar and Directorate of Marketing & Inspections (DMI), Ministry of Agriculture, Jammu was successfully conducted at Jammu from 24th March to 10th April 2003. DOEACC Chandigarh organized the programme in close coordination with NIC J&K State Unit. 20 participants (15 from CIPMC Jammu/Srinagar and 5 from DMI Jammu) attended the training. The training contents included Office Productivity Tools, Data Creation & Analysis and Decision Support Systems.
