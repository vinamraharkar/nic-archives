## System for PF accounting

The NIC Madhya Pradesh State Unit has developed a Departmental Provident Fund Accounting and Information System for maintaining the accounts of Departmental Provident Fund (DPF) contributions from Secretariat employees. This software is very generalized in nature and covers contributions, loans and part/final withdrawals.
