## Cyber Governance

A look at some of the recently launched Indian Government Websites….

Mumbai Airport Customs (http://customsmumbaiairport.gov.in/)
Mumbai airport is one of the major and busiest gateways to India. Hence the clearance of customs on arrival as well as departure has to be correctly informed to its stakeholders for a hassle free trip. This website caters to all the necessary information relevant to Mumbai Airport Customs as well as the customs & duty applicable at Mumbai Airport. Browse through it to access vital information on customs on arrival/ departure, airlines & crew, diplomats, air intelligence unit, domestic passengers along with necessary custom tariff and rules and regulations. The website also features host of important links, grievance assistance, right to information etc.

Madhya Pradesh Urja Vikash Nigam Limited (http://mprenewable.nic.in/ )
Madhya Pradesh government added a new feather to its renewable energy sector with the launch of MP Urja Vikash Nigam (MPUVN) Limited website. The website informs about all the necessary implementations, projects and sponsored programs taken by the Government for MPUVN. Besides it also creates awareness among the masses about the benefits of various forms of energy conservation and renewable energy. The website is also updated regularly with the latest relevant schemes and policy incentives.

NRI Puducherry, Deptt. of Industries and Commerce, (http://nri.puducherry.gov.in )
An exclusive website, made available by the Industries & Commerce Deptt. of Puducherry enables NRIs/PIOs/OCI to participate in industrial development of Puducherry. The website provides value-added service to French Nationals of Puducherry origin. Besides, serving as an interface between the French Nationals and the Puducherry administration, the website helps the NRIs from Puducherry to gather information about the various investment opportunities available here. Nevertheless, the website also renders a host of other informations of the union territory viz. tourism, culture, legislative, education etc. Register online to avail all the facilities.

Department of Pharmaceuticals, Govt. of India (http://pharmaceuticals.gov.in/ )
The website is built with the aim of helping Indian Pharmaceutical Industry to prosper in the global market ensuring abundant availability, at reasonable prices within the country, of good quality pharmaceuticals of mass consumption. The site is a one stop source for those who are interested in or already into the business of Pharmaceuticals. Stakeholders will find a slew of vital information viz. acts & policies, RTI Acts, plan schemes, production details, current inflammation scenario etc. It is hoped that the website will go a long way in its endeavors, in the coming future.
