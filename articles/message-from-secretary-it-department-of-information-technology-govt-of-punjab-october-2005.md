## Message from Secretary, IT, Department of Information Technology, Govt. of Punjab

I am happy to note that Informatics, Quarterly newsletter of NIC, is the source of sharing information on e-governance related projects in India. I am also of the opinion that NIC is providing best technical services for delivery of better services to the citizens and government by use of IT tools. It is team efforts of NIC and Punjab Government officers that we are marching forward in implementation of meaningful e-governance projects. I wish good luck to Editorial Team of Informatics and Punjab State NIC for the team under the able leadership of Mr. Sunil Kumar, SIO for commendable work toward e-Governance Projects in the state. Keep it up.
