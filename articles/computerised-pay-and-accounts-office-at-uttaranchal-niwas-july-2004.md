## Computerised Pay and Accounts Office at Uttaranchal Niwas

A computerised Pay and Accounts office at Uttaranchal Niwas, New Delhi was recently inaugurated by Chief Secretary, Uttaranchal, Dr. R S Tolia in presence of Additional Resident Commissioner, Uttaranchal Ms. Vineeta Kumar. NIC Uttaranchal has had a privilege to introduce the indispensable tool of computerization for formulating various financial transactions of government. This step will add one more node into the stream of computerized accounting system of Government of Uttaranchal.
