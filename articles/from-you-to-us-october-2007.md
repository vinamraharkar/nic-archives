## From You to Us

I am an avid reader of Informatics. The July issue took me for pleasant surprise, considering its excellent new format and new presentation style. I congratulate the entire team for this. One thing I always wish that since the Government is doing so much in the field of making IT people centric and people friendly, it is the high time that Informatics should be available for the public at large, in book stalls, libraries, panchyats etc. All ways and means should be adopted to bridge the digital divide, and for this Informatics can definitely play the role as agent of change. Again my best wishes to the entire team of Informatics. And I sincerely pray for many more successes.
Girish Sharma
Joint Collector
Raisen (Madhya Pradesh)

I have gone through the latest issue of Informatics magazine. It is very good in its new look, design and features covered. It is at par with any other popular IT magazine, in its presentation, content and volume. The new sections like International e-Gov Updates, Guest Column, Book Review etc. are quite useful to the regular readers. The Technology Update on Data Centre and IPV6 were very informative. The thumb nails of the authors of each article/section helps us, NICians to know the people who are contributing.
Please convey my greetings to the entire team who toiled hard to bring the new elegant look to our very own magazine.
Dr.T.R.Vijaya Kumar
NIC, Kuppam (Andhra Pradesh)
