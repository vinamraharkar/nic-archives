## Distt Jammu & Distt Budgam , J&K

Jammu and Budgam Districts in the state of Jammu and Kashmir became the latest to launch their own websites recently on NIC’s web server. The websites of the two districts carry comprehensive information about aspects such as administrative set-up, historical background , religious and tourist places etc. The Jammu District’s website was inaugurated by Dr, Farooq Abdullah, Hon’ble Chief Minister of Jammu & Kashmir and the Budgam District’s website was inaugurated at a special function by Sh. A.R Rather, Minister of Finance, Govt of Jammu & Kashmir.
