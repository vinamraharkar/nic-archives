## Registrar of Companies, Orissa went online

Registrar of Companies, Orissa went online with the launching of its website by Sh. Bibekananda Mohanty, ROC, Cuttack in presence of Sh. S K Panda, STD & SIO in the inaugural function attended by officials of ROC, Cuttack as well as NIC, OSU. The website provides a rich source of information and reference materials for citizens, investors as well as for the 7331 companies registered in Orissa so far. It has dynamic online query facility for company information, name availability, director’s information, filing status, change in name and default companies.
