## SESAM Workshop, Punjab

Workshop on Super Element Structural Analysis Modules software (SESAM) software to carry out Finite Element Analysis, Design and Detailing was organized by NIC Punjab. Mr R.S. Sandhu, Secretary (PWD), Punjab inaugurated the workshop. Chief engineers, executive engineers and other officers from the department participated in the workshop. Mr. P.V. Lakshminarayan, SrTD, CAD Group, NIC HQ demonstrated the SESAM software and carried out analysis for some of the Projects handled by PWD, Punjab. Shri Sandhu, Secretary (PWD), Govt. of Punjab appreciated the efforts of NIC in this sector.
