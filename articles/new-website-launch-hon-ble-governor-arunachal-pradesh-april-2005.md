## New Website Launch: Hon'ble Governor, Arunachal Pradesh

The website of Governor of Arunachal Pradesh was formally launched by Hon'ble Chief Minister, Shri Gegong Apang, on 26th Jan 2005, in the presence of the Hon'ble Governor, Chief Secretary, Legislators, civil and Police officers and other dignitaries. Inaugural function was hosted by H.E. the Governor of Arunachal Pradesh, Shri SK Singh at Raj Bhawan. In his message, the Governor thanked NIC Arunachal Pradesh State Unit for the design, development and hosting of the website. Other dignitaries attending the function also appreciated NIC for its efforts and contribution for implementation of the website.
