## Computerised Birth & Death Registration at Bhubaneswar

A Computerised Birth & Death Registration System, developed by NIC Orissa State Unit for the Bhubaneswar Municipal Corporation was inaugurated by Sh. Biswabhushan Harichandan, Hon’ble Minister, Revenue & Law, Govt of Orissa. Sh. Samir Dey, Hon’ble Minister, Urban Development presided over the meeting and declared that the government shall soon implement the package at all urban bodies. Sh. S.K Panda, SIO, highlighted the features of the software and a team of NIC officers led by Sh. A.K Hota presented the web enabled module of the software before the audience.
