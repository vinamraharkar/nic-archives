## Krishi Marata Vahini

Krishi Marata Vahini is a bi-lingual (Kannada and English) Regional portal developed by NIC Karnataka to strengthen the AGMARKNET Programme in the State. The Portal is a complete web enabled agricultural market information system covering all the markets in Karnataka State. The Portal was inaugurated by Hon’ble Chief Minister of Karnataka Sh S.M Krishna in presence of Dr. N. Vijayaditya, DG(NIC) and Sh. M. Moni, DDG (NIC). The portal gets updated directly from markets everyday and provides marketwise, commodity wise, daily and periodic report. Information on the status of updation and holiday details are also available on daily basis. Krishi Marata Vahini will be available through IVRS shortly.
