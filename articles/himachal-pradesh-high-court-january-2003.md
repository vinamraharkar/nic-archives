## Himachal Pradesh High Court

Himachal Pradesh High Court
http://himachal.nic.in/highcourt
From Ajay Chahal, Himachal Pradesh Correspondent
The High Court of Himachal Pradesh went online recently with the launch of its official website by the Hon’ble Chief Justice of HP High Court, Mr. W.A. Shishak. The website contains information about history of HP High Court, Administrative set-up, Legal Aid, District Courts, Judges profile (sitting and previous), telephone directory, rules, yearly calendar etc. The Monthly and Daily Cause List is also available. The website has been designed & developed by NIC Cell at HP High Court.
