## NICian tops Hindi Examination

Ms Sanjana Sharma, a stenographer posted at NIC HQ secured the first rank among hundreds of participants in the Hindi Stenography Examination conducted on a National level by Department of Official Language, Ministry of Home Affairs. She was awarded merit certificate for obtaining this distinction. Many other NIC staff members also participated in the course and obtained distinction marks, thus stressing on NIC’s continued commitment towards propagation of Government’s Official Language Policy.
