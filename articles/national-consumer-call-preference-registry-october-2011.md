## National Consumer Call Preference Registry

Hon'ble Union Minister of Communications and IT Sh. Kapil Sibal and Hon'ble MoS(C & IT) Sh. Sachin Pilot and Sh. Milind Deora launched the National Consumer Call Preference Registry (NCCPR) designed and developed by National Informatics Centre (NIC) for Telecom Regulatory Authority of India (TRAI) on 27/09/2011. NIC's role has been applauded during the launching ceremony.
