## Agriculture Department, Chhattisgarh

Hon’ble Chief Minister of Chhattisgarh, Dr. Raman Singh recently inaugurated the website of the departments of Agriculture, Cooperatives and Fisheries, developed under the guidance of NIC Chhattisgarh State Centre. Dr. N Vijayaditya, DG NIC was also present at the august function. The website provides exhaustive information, useful to public and department as well, related to agriculture, cooperatives and fisheries departments. Information about various schemes, products, license related information, informative photographs, links to important sites such as the Meteorological Department and downloadable forms makes the website complete in all respects.
