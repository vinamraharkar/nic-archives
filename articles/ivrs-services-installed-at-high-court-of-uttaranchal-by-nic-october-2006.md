## IVRS services installed at High Court of Uttaranchal by NIC

Interactive Voice Response System (IVRS) has successfully been installed in the High Court of Uttaranchal at Nainital. Using this system any one can enquire about the cases in the High Court through telephone lines (05942-235388 ext. 222 and 05942-233501) round the clock. The System was inaugurated by the Hon’ble Chief Justice of Uttranachal High Court Sh. Rajeev Gupta in the presence of several dignitaries. The system has been implemented with the overall support of NIC Uttaranchal State Centre
