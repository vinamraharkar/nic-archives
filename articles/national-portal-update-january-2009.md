## National Portal Update

'india.gov.in', the national portal of India provides single window access to information and online services for citizens and other stakeholders. The portal provides a unified interface to over 5000 Indian Government websites and is a mission mode project under the National e-Governance Plan (NeGP). It provides comprehensive, accurate, reliable and up-to-date information about India and its various facets with exclusive sections on Citizens, Business, overseas, Government, Know India Sectors etc.
