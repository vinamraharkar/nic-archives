## TRAINING PROGRAMMES FOR NIC OFFICIALS

New Delhi : The National Informatics Centre is, for the first time organizing a series of Training Programmes, to familiarize NIC professionals with MicroSoft (MS) Office Software and Lotus Smart Suite Software, at the NIC Headquarters, New Delhi.

The Programme on MS Office will cover MS Word; MS Access; MS Excel; MS Powerpoint and MS Outlook. The duration of the Programme will be five days and the scheduled dates are April 21-25; May 5-9 ; June 2-6.

The Programme on Lotus Smart Suite will cover Lotus Wordpro; Lotus Approach; Lotus 1-2-3 release V; Lotus Organizer; Lotus Freelance. The duration of this Programme will also be five days and the schedule is, April 28- May 2; May 26- 30; June 9-13.
