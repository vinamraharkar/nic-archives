## DG NIC Honored with ‘Digital Champion’ Award at Tech Sabha 2025

At the prestigious Tech Sabha 2025, Shri IPS Sethi, Director General, NIC was conferred with the “Digital Champion” Award, acknowledging his visionary leadership and transformative contributions to digital governance. His efforts in spearheading cutting-edge technology solutions have significantly advanced the nation’s digital infrastructure, making governance more accessible, efficient, and citizen-centric.
The Digital Champion Award, a hallmark of excellence in e-Governance and technology-driven public service, was presented to Shri Sethi for his innovative approach in driving digital reforms, ensuring seamless service delivery, and enhancing transparency across government platforms.
Under his leadership, several flagship digital initiatives have been successfully implemented, paving the way for smart governance frameworks, AI-driven public services, and enhanced digital inclusivity. His contributions have played a pivotal role in transforming citizen engagement, data-driven policymaking, and secure digital ecosystems within governance structures.
