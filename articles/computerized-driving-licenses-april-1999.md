## Computerized Driving Licenses

The Pondicherry Unit of NIC has developed a software for the computerization of Driving License and Registration Certificates of vehicles in Pondicherry. At a specially held function in Pondicherry, the Lt.Governor of Pondicherry Dr.(Smt.) Rajani Rai inaugurated the issue of computerized licenses to the general public. For the implementation of the software, the Transport Wings of the various Districts of Pondicherry have been connected through NICNET with NIC Pondicherry to get the necessary information related to registered vehicles, licenses etc.

Hon'ble Lt.Governor of Pondicherry, Smt. Rajani Rai, inaugurating the issue of computerized driving licenses.
