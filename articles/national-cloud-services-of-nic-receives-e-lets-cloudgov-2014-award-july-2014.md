## NATIONAL CLOUD SERVICES OF NIC RECEIVES E-LETS CLOUDGOV 2014 AWARD

National Cloud of NIC (MeghRaj Cloud Initiative) has recently bagged the prestigious elets CloudGov Award for the year 2014 under the category - “Best Cloud Deployment in Government Sector”.

The award was received by Shri Vijay Kumar Vishwakarma (Technical Director) from Shri R.S. Sharma, Secretary, DeitY, Ministry of C&IT, in the presence of other dignitaries in the function held at The Royal Plaza, Ashoka Road, New Delhi on 10th June 2014. National Cloud is a state-of-the-art secure Government Cloud, setup up by NIC, providing services over the ICT infrastructure spanning its National Data Centres. The Cloud Services available are Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS), Platform as a Service (PaaS), Software as a Service (SaaS) and Storage as a Service (STaaS). NIC is providing Cloud services under the umbrella of MeghRaj through https://cloud.gov.in.
