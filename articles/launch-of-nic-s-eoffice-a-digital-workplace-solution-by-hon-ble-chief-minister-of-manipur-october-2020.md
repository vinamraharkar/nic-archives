## Launch of NIC’s eOffice, a Digital Workplace Solution, by Hon’ble Chief Minister of Manipur

Hon’ble Chief Minister Manipur Shri N. Biren Singh launched NIC’s eOffice ‘A digital workplace solution’. It is a step towards a paperless office, bringing ease of Governance with transparency & accountability.
