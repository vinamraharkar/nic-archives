## Website of ROC Gujarat, Dadra & Nagar Haveli launched

The website of Registrar of Companies, Gujarat, Dadra & Nagar Haveli that has been designed, developed and hosted by NIC, was launched recently. The website provides comprehensive information related to the department including Name Availability (for forming a new company or for changing the name of existing company), New Incorporation, Guidelines for Filing of Returns, Schedule of Fees, List of Registered Companies, Companies in Liquidation, etc.
