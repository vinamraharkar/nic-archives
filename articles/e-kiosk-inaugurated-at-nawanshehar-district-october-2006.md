## E – kiosk inaugurated at Nawanshehar District

Sh.Rajan Kashyap, Hon’ble State Chief Information Commissioner Punjab recently inaugurated E-Kiosk at Nawanshehar district . E-Kiosk is a touch screen based computerized information dissemination system implemented at Nawanshehar district. E-Kiosk is conceived to disseminate the information regarding SUWIDHA status, Voters list, various schemes related to development, health etc. The software for E-Kiosk has been designed and developed by NIC Punjab with the active support and guidance of Deputy Commissioner Nawanshehar. Being touch screen based system, it is very citizen friendly.
