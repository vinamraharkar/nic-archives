## Online publication of Jharkhand Academic Council Results

The Jharkhand Academic Council declared the examination results for the year 2005 on the website http://jharresults.nic.in. The matric result was launched by Sh. Arjun Munda, Hon’ble Chief Minister, Jharkhand. The results for Intermediate Science and Arts were launched by the State HRD Minister. After the declaration of the results, students who had already registered themselves on the website, received their results automatically through email. The results website has been designed and developed by NIC Jharkhand State Centre and handled more than three lakh hits within 24 hours of its release.
