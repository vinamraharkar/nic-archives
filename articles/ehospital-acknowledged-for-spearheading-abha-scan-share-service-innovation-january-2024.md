## eHospital Acknowledged for Spearheading ABHA ‘Scan & Share’ Service Innovation

eHospital platform developed by the National Informatics Centre (NIC) has been recognized with a Certificate of Appreciation for its pioneering role in implementing and launching the Ayushman Bharat Health Account (ABHA) ‘Scan & Share’ Service by the National Health Authority. This innovative service utilises Quick Response (QR) codes integrated into health facilities to streamline patient registration processes, effectively reducing long queues and ensuring precise data entry. The implementation of this technology marks a significant advancement in enhancing efficiency within healthcare facilities, allowing for smoother operations and improved accuracy in patient information management.
