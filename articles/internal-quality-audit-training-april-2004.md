## Internal Quality Audit Training

STQC is conducting training Courses on Internal Quality Audit (IQA) for NIC Officers with regards to ISO 9001:2000 Certification Processes in NIC. Each Course is of 2 day duration and a test conducted at the end of the course qualifies the ‘Internal Quality Auditors’. The first training was held on December 2nd and 3rd at NIC HQ and the successful participants were awarded Certificates of Indian Institute of Quality Management, Jaipur distributed by DG(NIC).
