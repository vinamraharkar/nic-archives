## VEHICLE REGISTRATION

Sikkim: The National Informatics Centre, Sikkim State Unit has successfully developed and implemented a Motor Vehicle Registration Software, for the Motor Vehicle Department, Government of Sikkim.

The off-line data-entry work for over 10,000 registrations has been completed. The Department is now planning to implement the on-line system for the same.
