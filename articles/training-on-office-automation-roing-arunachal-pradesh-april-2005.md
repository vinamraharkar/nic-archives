## Training on Office Automation, Roing, Arunachal Pradesh

A two-week training on 'Office Automation' was inaugurated by Dr. BS Banerjee, Deputy Commissioner, Lower Dibang Valley district, Roing recently. The training was sponsored by the Department of Personnel, Training and Administrative Reforms, Govt of India, New Delhi and Administrative Training Institute (ATI), Naharlagun. It was organised for the officers and ministerial staff of Lower Dibang Valley district. Around 22 trainees including officers and ministerial staff attended the programme and benefited from it.
