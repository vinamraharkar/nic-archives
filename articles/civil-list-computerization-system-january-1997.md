## Civil List Computerization System

In view of the availability of NICNET throughout Rajasthan including all District Headquarters and Rajasthan Secretariat Campus, Jaipur, it was felt that this wide network could be used to effectively disseminate the latest detailed information about civil services officers such as IAS, IPS, IFS and RAS. For this purpose CLICS (Civil List Computerization System) is designed and implemented on NICNET by Rajasthan State Unit of National Informatics Centre, while current data updates are provided by Department of Personnel (DOP), Government of Rajasthan. One can get complete details of a particular officer or a list of officers or for officers of a complete cadre either on screen or in print. It provides a wide range of queries, multiple queries, reports and summary reports on civil lists of Rajasthan. The Software has been developed in ORACLE.
