## NIC participation at International Conference of Medical Librarians

Smt. Naina Pandita, Scientist ‘F’, NIC HQ was a part of the panel for Open Access for Developing Countries in the International Conference of Medical Librarians (IMCL9) held in September 2005 at Salvador, Brazil. She made a presentation on “Open Access Journals in India”. The group formulated the “Salvador Declaration on Open Access: The Developing World Perspective”.
