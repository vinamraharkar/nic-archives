## PANI

PANI mobile app is developed analogous to Portal for Assets and Navigational Information (PANI) system. It aims to provide transparency in river navigation and infrastructure information for making timely decisions based on a single source of truth and facilitate the transportation of cargo and other vessels on inland waterways.
The app provides detailed information on the National Waterways and their terminal assets, including fairway, basic infrastructure (jetties, pontoons, cargo handling equipment, and storage facilities), cross river structures (bridge locations, locks, and barrages), connectivity at jetties, and emergency services to facilitate the transportation of cargo and other vessels through inland waterways.
The app uses GIS-based Bharat maps to facilitate visual comprehension of the above information and navigation. By simulating historical data against required draught, vertical and horizontal clearance, it assists users in evaluating the viability of transportation on a planned date of travel.
Thus, the app allows internal stakeholders to make well-informed and timely decisions and facilitates business on inland waterways by providing a single point of contact for voyage planning and authorization.
