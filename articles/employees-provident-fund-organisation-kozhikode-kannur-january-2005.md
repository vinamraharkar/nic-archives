## Employees Provident Fund Organisation, Kozhikode & Kannur

The website for the Employees Provident Fund Organisation, Kozhikode and Kannur, developed with the support of NITPU, Cochin, provides important information regarding the application status of an employee besides enabling a person to find his/her balance account. Other important information being provided on the site are calculation of pension, remittance status, list of defaulters etc.
