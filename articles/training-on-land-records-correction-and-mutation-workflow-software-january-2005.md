## Training on Land Records Correction and Mutation/workflow Software

A six-day training programme on Land Records Correction and Mutation/ Workflow software was organized for Revenue Department staff at Kakinada, East Godavari District, Andhra Pradesh. Over 100 staff members were trained for six days. District administration has lauded the efforts of NIC East Godavari District.
