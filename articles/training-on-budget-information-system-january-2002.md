## Training on Budget Information System

The Communication Informatics Division of NIC has developed a Budget Information System to computerize the
budget work in the various Postal Circles in Delhi. A training was conducted at Dak Bhawan, Department of Posts,
recently to apprise the officers about the new system. Various Officers from the Budget and Book section of the
Department attended the training programme.
