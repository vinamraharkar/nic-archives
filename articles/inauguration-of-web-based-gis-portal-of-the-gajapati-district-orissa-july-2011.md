## Inauguration of Web based GIS Portal of the Gajapati District, Orissa

Recently NIC District Centre Gajapati moved into its new building duly inaugrated by Sh. P.C. Das, District Collector. On this occasion, the GIS portal http://gis.ori.nic.in/orissanew/gajapati/frontpage/default.htm, specifically designed for Gajapati district under guidance of Dr. Manjurani Routray, Scientist-E & Head, GIS Division, NIC Orissa State Unit, was inaugurated by District Collector. Sh. Bijaya Kumar Samal, Scientist-D, GIS Div., NIC Orissa presented various features of the Web-GIS to all district heads. Role based user credentials have been provided to the district administration for updation of the database on the server. The programme was coordinated by Sh. T. Balakrishna Murty, DIO & Scientist-C & Sh. R. R. Majhi, S.O/Engr-SB.
