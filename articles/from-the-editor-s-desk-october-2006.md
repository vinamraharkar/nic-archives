## From the Editor's Desk

The festive month of October ended on a significant note marking a shift in the leadership of NIC …with Dr N. Vijayaditya superannuating after a distinguished and memorable stint as Director General and Dr B.K Gairola taking over as the new DG, NIC. The NICians at the Headquarters and every nook and corner of the Country bid adieu to Dr N.Vijayaditya and thanked him for leading NIC to such great heights under his able guidance. On November 1st, we all heartily welcomed our new DG, Dr Gairola and wished him the very best for a glorious tenure ahead. We hope that Dr Gairola’s exemplary vision shall make NIC grow and nurture further from strength to strength.
