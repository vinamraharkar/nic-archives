## ICT Trainings Conducted by NIC at IAS Academy, Mussoorie

NIC Training Unit at Lal Bahadur Shastri National Academy Mussoorie regularly provides ICT related training to the officers of All India Services during all the training programmes conducted at the Academy. A synopsis of all the training programme conducted during the year 2006 reflects that a total of 566 officers were trained in various aspects of ICT including Internet, Email, Office tools, GIS, Data analysis etc during various courses lasting 286 sessions. The administration of the Academy is highly appreciative of the active support being provided by NIC in training the Civil Servants in the nuances of Information & Communication Technologies.
