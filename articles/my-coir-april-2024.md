## My Coir

My Coir app serves as an invaluable resource for entrepreneurs, particularly small busi-nesses, who are beneficiaries of the Coir Board's various schemes aimed at improving the socio-economic conditions of coir workers. This app provides a concise overview of the Coir Beneficiary Schemes implemented by the Coir Board, under the Ministry of MSME, Government of India including Coir Udyami Yojana (CUY), and Scheme of Fund for Re-generation of Traditional Industries (SFURTI).
Entrepreneurs can use the My Coir app to access detailed descriptions of the different Coir Beneficia-ry Schemes, helping them understand the available opportunities for support and assistance. Addition-ally, the app allows beneficiaries to track the status of their submitted applications, providing transpar-ency and clarity throughout the process.
By centralizing information and streamlining communication between the Coir Board and ben-eficiaries, My Coir app empowers entrepreneurs to make informed decisions and navigate the various schemes effectively. Ultimately, it plays a vital role in facilitating access to resources and fostering growth within the coir industry, contributing to the socio-economic development of coir workers and small businesses across India
