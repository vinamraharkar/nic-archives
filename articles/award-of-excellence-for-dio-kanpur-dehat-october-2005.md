## Award of Excellence for DIO, Kanpur Dehat

Sh. Alok Tiwari, PSA and DIO, Kanpur Dehat has been awarded the ‘Utkrishta Karmi Samman 2005’ for his dedication and exemplary services in the field of IT in the District. At the felicitation ceremony, Sh. A K Goel, District Magistrate of Kanpur Dehat appreciated the excellent contribution of Mr. Tiwari in implementing various e-governance projects in the district and presented him a memento and an appreciation letter.
