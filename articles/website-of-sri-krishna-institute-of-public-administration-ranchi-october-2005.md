## Website of Sri Krishna Institute of Public Administration, Ranchi

Website of Sri Krishna Institute of Public Administration (SKIPA), Ranchi that has been designed and developed by NIC Jharkhand State Centre was launched on 27th August by Smt. Laxmi Singh, Director General, SKIPA. The senior officials and staff of the institute were present on the occasion. The website provides an overview of the institute and has details about various training programmes run by it.
