## Letters to the Editor

Dear Editor,
I have been reading Informatics, NIC’s E-Governance Bulletin since July 2003. It carries an accelerated approach towards E-Governance. It has sowed seeds of E-Governance. Inspite of our remote and interior location ‘Informatics’ has inspired us to start Computerisation of Land Records of the District and it is likely to be completed soon. Thanks to the ‘Informatics’ team for bringing out the magazine & enriching our knowledge.
K.K. Chhetry
Deputy Commissioner,
Imphal East District
Dear Editor,
The issues of Informatics E-Governance Bulletin are regularly sent to me by State Informatics Officer, Goa. I find the magazine very informative. I feel that Knowledge shared is Knowledge Integrated. This effort of NIC will definitely reduce a lot of duplication work in the ICT field in States and Central Government Departments. The products developed by various centers of NIC can be utilized by other state units. I feel that you must also focus on the products replicated by various other state units. If replication process takes place, a lot of state governments will have good e-Governance projects in place within a short span of time.
M N RAO
Advisor & Director (IT)
Department of Information Technology
Government of Goa
