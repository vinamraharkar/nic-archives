## Investment Opportunities and Incentives Corner

India is emerging as one of the fastest growing economic zones in the world. “Investment Opportunities and Incentives” section has been added recently under Business module on the National Portal. The section answers all business and investment related queries in our country. From Government Funding schemes to private financial institutions, India has a wide platter of business financing options to choose from. This exclusive section gives updated and unbiased information about the new and lucrative business avenues available in the country.
